====== Jonathan ======

===== Facts: =====

The name "Jonathan" occurs many times in the Old Testament. It means, "Yahweh has given."

  * The most well-known man named Jonathan was the oldest son of King Saul. He was David's best friend.
  * Other Jonathans mentioned in the Old Testament include: a grandson of Moses; a nephew of King David; <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>a son of Abiathar the priest, who served David;</font> a secretary in whose house the prophet Jeremiah was imprisoned; and many others..
