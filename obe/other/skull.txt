====== skull ======

===== Definition: =====

A skull is the bony, skeletal structure of a person's head.

  * Animals also have skulls.
  * The "Place of the Skull" was another name for Golgotha where Jesus was crucified.

(See also: [[:En:obe:other:Golgotha]])
