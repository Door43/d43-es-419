====== mighty, might ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "mighty" describes someone or something that is strong and powerful.

  * A mighty person could also be described as "having great might" or "having great power and strength." 
  * In the Old Testament, "mighty" especially describes a man who is victorious in battle.
  * In Acts 7, Moses is described as a man who was "mighty in word and deed.". This could be translated as, "Moses gave powerful teachings and did miraculous things" or "Moses spoke God's word powerfully and did many amazing things."
  * The term "might" could also be translated as "power" or "great strength."
  * The term "Almighty" is a common description for God which means he has complete power to do miracles and anything else he wants to do.

(See also: [[:es-419:obe:kt:Almighty]], [[:es-419:obe:kt:miracle]], [[:es-419:obe:kt:power]])
