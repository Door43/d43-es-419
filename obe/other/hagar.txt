====== Hagar (Agar)======


===== Facts: =====

Hagar was Sarai's personal slave.

  * Sarai, later named Sarah, gave Hagar to her husband, Abram, as a slave wife.
  * Sarai wanted Hagar to bear children for her and Abram.
  * Hagar gave birth to Abram's son, Ishmael.

(Translation suggestions: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

(See also: [[sarah]], [[en:obe:other:abraham]], [[servant]])


Agar era la esclava personal de Saraí.

  * Saraí, luego llamada Sara, le dió a Agar a su esposo Abram, como su esposa esclava.
  * Saraí quiso que Agar tuviera un niño para ella y Abram.
  * Agar dió a luz el hijo de Abram, Ismael.
