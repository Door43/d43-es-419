====== acquit ======

====== absolver ======

===== Definition: =====

The word "acquit" is a legal term that means to formally declare someone to not be guilty of an unlawful activity.

La palabra 'absolver' es el término legal que significa formalmente declarar a alguien no ser culpable de un activity ilegal.

  * This term is sometimes used in the Bible to talk about forgiving sinners.
  * Este término es aveces utilizado en la Biblia para hablar sobre perdonar pecadores.
  * Often the context is about wrongly acquitting people who continue to rebel against God.
  * A menudo el contexto es sobre absolver erróneamente a gente que continua rebelándose contra Dios.
  * This could be translated as, "declare innocent" or "judge to be not guilty."
  * Esto podría ser traducido como, 'declara inocente' o 'juzgado no ser culpable'.

(See also: [[:es-419:obe:kt:forgive|forgive, forgiveness (perdonar, perdón)]], [[:es-419:obe:kt:guilt|guilt, guilty (culpa, culpable)]])
