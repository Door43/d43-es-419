====== gold ======

===== Definition: =====

Gold is a yellow-colored precious metal that is among the oldest known to mankind.

  * Gold is found in a pure state in nature and is of a long-lasting, high quality.
  * Many things were made out of gold, including idols, jewelry, altars, parts of the ark and the temple.
  * Sometimes when something is described as "gold" it means that it has a gold overlay but is not solid gold. In those cases a word like "golden" or "gold-overlaid" could be used.
