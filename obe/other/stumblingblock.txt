====== stumbling block, stone of stumbling ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "stumbling block" or "stone of stumbling" literally refers to a physical object that causes a person to trip and fall.

  * A figurative stumbling block is anything that causes a person to fail in a moral or spiritual sense.
  * Also in a figurative sense, a "stumbling block" or a "stone of stumbling" is something that prevents someone from having faith in Jesus or that causes someone to not grow spiritually.
  * Often it is sin that acts as a stumbling block to oneself or to others.
  * One word translated as "stumbling block" means "trap" or "snare."
  * This term could also be translated as, "obstacle" or "something that causes someone to sin."
