====== submit, in submission ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "submit" means to voluntarily place oneelf under the authority of a person or government.

  * The Biblet tells believers in Jesus to submit to God.
  * They are also told to submit to each other, putting each others' needs above their own.
  * Wives are specifically told to submit to their husbands and to willingly live under their authority.
  * Slaves are told to submit to their masters and obey them.
  * The Bible also instructs believers in Christ to obey the government that rules their country. Of course, if the government makes a law that would cause disobedience to God, then a believer must nof obey that law..
  * To "live in submission to" something or someone means to live in a way that does what that authority wants.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * The term "submit" coudl also be translated as, "put under the authority of" or "follow the leadership of."
  * "live in submission to" could be translated as, "ldo things that obey and honor."
