====== Succoth ======

===== Definition: =====

Succoth is the name of two Old Testament cities.

  * The first Succoth is mentioned as a place where the Israelites stopped on their way out of Egypt during the exodus. It is located on the norther border of Egypt.
  * The second mention of Succoth is a city on the east side of the Jordan river. Gideon is said to have stopped there with his men.
