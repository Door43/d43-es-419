====== fellowship offering ======

===== Facts: =====

In the Old Testament book of Leviticus, the fellowship offering is also called the peace offering.

  * The offering could be for thanksgiving, to complete a vow, or as a freewill offering.
  * Unlike many sacrifices, the peace offering was shared with God, priests and the donor.
  * There was a meal associated with this offering which included unleavened bread.
