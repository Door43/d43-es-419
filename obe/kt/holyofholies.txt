====== most holy place ======

===== Definition: =====

Inside the Israelite tabernacle or temple building, the "most holy place" was the innermost chamber where the Ark of God was located, symbolizing God's presence.

  * The high priest was the only person allowed into the Most Holy Place. He was permitted to enter it one time every year, on the day of atonement.
  * On the day of atonement, the high priest would offer incense on the altar and sprinkle with blood the cover of the ark of the covenant.
  * The "Most Holy Place" represents the inner access to the presence of God where Jesus' own blood is the only acceptable sacrifice for sin.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * This term could also be translated as, "holiest room" or "holiest place where God lives" or "most sacred chamber set apart for God."
  * Compare the translation of this term with the translation of holy place.
