====== veil ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "veil" usually refers to a tnin piece of cloth that was used as a head covering to cover the head or face so that it cannot be seen.

  * Moses covered his face with a veil after he had been in the presence of Yahweh, so that the brightness of his face would be hidden from the people.
  * In the Bible women wore a veil to cover their head and often their face as well when they were in public or in the presence of men.
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>T</font> <font 14px line-height: 19.6px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>he verb "to veil" means to cover something with a veil.</font>
  * In some English versions, the word "veil" is used to refer to the thick curtain that covered the entrance into the most holy place. But "curtain' is a more accurate term since it refers to a heavy, thick piece of cloth.
