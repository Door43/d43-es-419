====== Lord's Supper ======

===== Definition: =====

The New Testament church practice also called "communion."

  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>This is based upon the "last supper" or passover meal Jesus shared with His disciples on the night he was betrayed.</font>
  * Jesus gave His disciples the bread and cup to share, and He set it as a church ordinance when He said "as often as you do this you do it in remembrance of Me."
  * The Apostle Paul also further establishes it as a believer's practice in 1 Corinthians chapter 11.
