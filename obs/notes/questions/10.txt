====== Story #10: The Ten Plagues: Les dix plaies ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers; Les questions de comprehensions et Reponses =====

  - **What was God's message that Moses and Aaron gave to Pharaoh?**
  - Quel a ete le message de Dieu que Moise et Aaron avaient donne a Pharaon
      * //"Let my people go!" [10-01]//
      * //Laissez mon peuple partir//
  - **What did Pharaoh do when he heard this command?**
  - qu'est ce que Pharaon avait fait quand il a entendu cet ordre
      * //He forced the Israelites to work even harder. [10-01]////Il avait force a travaille tres dur//
  - **What did God do when Pharaoh refused to let the Israelites go?: Qu'est que Dieu fait quand Pharaon a refuse de laisser les Israelites partir **
      * //He sent ten terrible plagues on Egypt. [10-02]:Il a envoye les dix terribles plaies en Egypte//
  - **What did God show Pharaoh through these plagues?: Qu'est ce que Dieu a montre a Pharaon par ces plaies**
      * //That he is more powerful than Pharaoh and all of Egypt's gods. [10-02]:Qu'il est plus puissant que Pharaon dieux et tous les Dieux d'Egypte//
  - **What did God do to the water in the Nile River?: Qu'est ce que Dieu a fait aux eaux de la riviere Nil**
      * //God turned water in the Nile River into blood. [10-03] Dieu a change les eaux de la riviere Nil en sang //
  - **Who was affected by the plague of the painful skin sores?:Qui a etait affecte par les douloureuses plaies sur leur peau **
      * //The sores appeared on the Egyptians, but not on the Israelites. [10-07].//
      * //Les douloureuses plaies apparaissent sur les egyptiens.mais pas sur les Israelites//
  - **Did the plague of darkness affect everyone equally?**
  - Les plaises de norceur affecte t-elle chacun de meme maniere
      * //No. It was dark where the Egyptians lived, but there was light where the Israelites lived. [10-11]//
  - **How did Pharaoh respond to each of the first nine plagues?**
      * //He refused to let the people go free. [10-12]//
  - **What did God do after Pharaoh did not respond to the first nine plagues?**
      * //God planned to send one last plague. [10-12]//
  - **What would this last plague do that the first nine had not done?**
      * //It would change Pharaoh's mind. [10-12]//
