====== obey  obedecer ======

===== Definition:  =====

**Definición**:

To obey is to follow the law or to do what someone in authority commands.

**Obedecer es seguir las leyes o hacer lo que alguien en autoridad ordena.**

   * When the law or someone in authority commands people to do something, they obey by doing it.
  * **Cuando una ley o alguien en autoridad ordena a las personas a hacer algo, ellos obedecen haciéndolo.**
  * When the law or someone in authority commands people not to do something, they obey by not doing it.
  * **Cuando la ley o alguien en autoridad ordena a la gente no hacer algo, ellos obedecen no haciéndolo.**
The only person who completely obeyed God was Jesus Christ, and him alone. The opposite of "obey" is "disobey."

**La única persona que obedeció completamente a Dios fue Jesucristo y sólo Él.  Lo opuesto a "obedecer" es "desobedecer".**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:03-04|[03-04]]]** Noah **obeyed**  God. He and his three sons built the boat just the way God had told them.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:05-06|[05-06]]]** Again Abraham **obeyed**  God and prepared to sacrifice his son.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:05-10|[05-10]]]**"Because you (Abraham) have **obeyed **  me, all the families of the world will be blessed through your family"
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:05-10|[11-06]]]** But the Egyptians did not believe God or **obey**  his commands.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:13-07|[13-07]]]** If the people **obeyed**  these laws, God promised that he would bless and protect them.
