====== altar __//altar//__ ======

===== Definition: =====

===== Definición: =====

An altar is a raised structure on which sacrifices were burned as gifts, or offerings, to God.

**Un altar es una estructura levantada en la cual se quemaban sacrificios como regalos u ofrendas a Dios.**

   * Altars were often made by packing together a mound of earth or by carefully placing stones into a large pile.
  * **A menudo, los altares se construían formando una colina de tierra o poniendo piedras cuidadosamente en una gran pila.**
  * Some special altars were made of metal, such as gold or bronze.
  * **Algúnos altares especiales eran hechos de metal, como oro o bronze.**
  * God told his people to build altars on which to make sacrifices to him, and the nations around Israel also built altars to make sacrifices to their gods.
  * **Dios le dijo a su pueblo que construyeran altares en los cuales pudieran hacer sacrificios, y las naciones alrededor de Israel también hicieron altares para hacer sacrificios a sus dioses.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:03-14|[03-14]]]** After Noah got off the boat, he built an **altar**  and sacrificed some of each kind of animal which could be used for a sacrifice.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:05-08|[05-08]]]** When they reached the place of sacrifice, Abraham tied up his son Isaac and laid him on an **altar**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:13-09|[13-09]]]** A priest would kill the animal and burn it on the **altar**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:16-06|[16-06]]]** He (Gideon) built a new altar dedicated to God near where the **altar**  to the idol used to be and made a sacrifice to God on it.
