====== Uriah //__Urias p__ //  ======

===== Facts: Datos =====

Uriah was a righteous man and one of King David's best soldiers.

\\
**Urías era un hombre justo y uno de los soldados del Rey David.**

   * Uriah had a very beautiful wife named Bathsheba.
  * **Urías tenía una esposa hermosa llamada Betsabé.**
  * David committed adultery with Uriah's wife, and she became pregnant with David's child.
  * **David cometió adulterio con la esposa de Urías y ella quedó embarazada de David.**
  * David caused Uriah to be killed in battle in order to cover up his sin. David then married Bathsheba.
  * **David causó la muerte de Urías colocándolo en el frente de la batalla para que lo mataran y así cubrir su pecado. David entonces se caso con Betsabé.**
[See also: **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:david|David]]**, **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:bathsheba|Bathsheba]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-12|[17-12]]]** Bathsheba's husband, a man named **Uriah**, was one of David's best soldiers. David called **Uriah**  back from the battle and told him to go be with his wife. But **Uriah**  refused to go home while the rest of the soldiers were in battle. So David sent **Uriah**  back to the battle and told the general to place him where the enemy was strongest so that he would be killed.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** After **Uriah**  was killed, David married Bathsheba.
