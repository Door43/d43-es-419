====== worship  culto ======

===== Definition: =====

"To worship" means to honor, praise and obey someone, especially God.

**"Rendir culto" significa honrar, alabar y obedecer a alguien, especialmente a Dios.**

   * We worship God when we serve and honor him, by praising him and obeying him.
  * **Rendimos culto a Dios cuando le servimos y le honramos, alabándolo y obedeciéndole.**
  * When the Israelites worshiped God, it often included sacrificing an animal on an altar.
  * **Cuando los Israelitas alababan a Dios, usualmente incluían el sacrificio de un animal en el altar.**
  * Some people worship false gods.
  * **Algunas personas rinden culto a falsos dioses.**
