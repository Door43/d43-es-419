====== Satan  Satanás ======

==== (Satan, devil, evil one) ====

(Satanás, diablo, el maligno)

===== Facts: =====

Datos:

Satan is a spirit being that God created, but he rebelled against God and became God's enemy.

**Satanás es un ser espiritual creado por Dios, pero se rebeló con tra Dios y se convirtió en enemigo de Dios.**

   * Satan hates God and all that God created, because he wants to take the place of God and be worshiped as God.
  * **Satanás odia a Dios y todo lo que Dios ha creado, porque el quiere ocupar el lugar de Dios y ser adorado como Dios.**
  * Satan tempts people to rebel against God so that he can become like a king over them.
  * **Satanás tienta a las personas a que se rebelen contra Dios para el convertirse en un rey sobre ellos.**
  * But God sent the Son of God to rescue people from Satan's control and bring them into God's kingdom.
  * **Pero Dios envió a su Hijo a rescatar a las personas del control de Satanás y traerlos al Reino de Dios.**
  * Satan is powerful, but God has all power, and one day the Son of God will completely destroy Satan.
  * **Satanás es poderoso, pero Dios tiene todo poder y un día el Hijo de Dios destruirá por completo a Satanás.**
  * Satan is also called, "the devil" and, "the evil one."
  * **Satanás es también llamado, "el diablo" y el "maligno"**
  * The name "Satan" means "enemy." The word "devil" means "accuser."
  * **El nombre de "Satanás" signigica "enemigo".  La palabra "diablo" significa"el acusador".**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:21-01|[21-01]]]** The snake who deceived Eve was **Satan**. The promise meant that the Messiah who would come would defeat **Satan**  completely.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:25-06|[25-06]]]** Then **Satan**  showed Jesus all the kingdoms of the world and all their glory and said, "I will give you all this if you bow down and worship me."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:25-08|[25-08]]]** Jesus did not give in to **Satan's**  temptations, so **Satan**  left him.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:33-06|[33-06]]]** So Jesus explained, "The seed is the word of God. The path is a person who hears God's word, but does not understand it, and the **devil**  takes the word from him."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:38-07|[38-07]]]** After Judas took the bread, **Satan**  entered into him.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:48-04|[48-04]]]** God promised that one of Eve's descendants would crush **Satan's**  head, and **Satan**  would wound his heel. This meant that **Satan**  would kill the Messiah, but God would raise him to life again, and then the Messiah will crush the power of **Satan**  forever.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:49-15|[49-15]]]** God has taken you out of **Satan's**  kingdom of darkness and put you into God's kingdom of light.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:50-09|[50-09]]]**"The weeds represent the people who belong to the **evil one**. The enemy who planted the weeds represents the **devil**."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:50-10|[50-10]]]**"When the world ends, the angels will gather together all the people who belong to the **devil**  and throw them into a raging fire, where they will cry and grind their teeth in terrible suffering."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:50-15|[50-15]]]** When Jesus returns, he will completely destroy **Satan**  and his kingdom. He will throw **Satan**  into hell where he will burn forever, along with everyone who chose to follow him rather than to obey God.
