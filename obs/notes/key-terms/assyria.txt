====== Assyria Asiria B ======

==== (Assyria, Assyrian, Assyrian Empire) ====

(Asiria, Imperio Asirio)

===== Facts: =====

**Datos:**

Assyria was a powerful empire during much of the time that the Israelites were living in the land of Canaan.

**Asiria era un poderoso imperio, durante gran parte del tiempo en que los Israelistas estaban viviendo en la tierra de Canaán.**

   * Assyria was located in an area that is today the north-central section of Iraq.
  * **Asiria estaba localizada en el área que es hoy la sección central-norte de Irak.**
  * The Assyrians fought against Israel at different times in their history. This was one way that God punished his people for rebelling against him and worshiping idols.
  * **Los Asirios pelearon en contra de Israel en diferentes ocasiones durante su historia. Esta fue una forma en que Dios castigó su gente por rebelarse en contra de Él rindiendo culto a idolos.**
  * In the year 722 BC, the Assyrians completely conquered the kingdom of Israel and forced many of the Israelites to leave Israel and move to Assyria.
  * **En el año 722 AC, los Asirios conquistaron completamente al reino de Israel y forzaron a muchos de los Israelistas a dejar Israel y mudarse de Asiria.**
  * The Assyrians also brought in many foreigners to intermarry with the Israelites who remained in Israel. The descendants of those people who intermarried were later called the Samaritans.
  * **Los Asirios trajeron muchos extranjeros para que se casaran con los Israelistas que quedaron en Israel. Los descendientes de éstos que se casaron fueron despues llamados Samaritanos.**
