====== Saul  Saúl ======

===== Facts: =====

Datos:

Saul was an Israelite man whom God chose to become the first king of Israel.

**Saúl fue un hombre israelita que Dios escogió para convertirse en el primer rey de Israel.**

   * Saul was tall and handsome, and a powerful soldier. He was the kind of person that the Israelites wanted to be their king.
  * **Saúl****era algo y guapo, y era un soldado poderoso.  El era la clase de persona que los Israelitas querían para que fuera su rey.**
  * Although he served God at first, he later became proud and disobeyed God. As a result, God appointed David to take Saul's place as king.
  * **A pesar de que el le sirvio a Dios al principio, luego se convirtió en un orgulloso y desobediente a Dios.  Como resultado, Dios señalo a David para tomar el lugar de Saúl como rey.**
Hundreds of years later, there was another Jew named Saul, but his name was changed to Paul after he came to know Jesus.

** Cientos de años más tarde, hubo otro judío llamado Saúl, pero su nombre fue cambiado a Pablo despúes de conocer a Jesús.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-01|[17-01]]]****Saul**  was the first king of Israel. He was tall and handsome, just like the people wanted. **Saul**  was a good king for the first few years that he ruled over Israel. But then he became a wicked man who did not obey God, so God chose a different man who would one day be king in his place.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-04|[17-04]]]****Saul**  became jealous of the people's love for David. **Saul**  tried many times to kill him, so David hid from **Saul**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-05|[17-05]]]** Eventually, **Saul**  died in battle, and David became king of Israel.
