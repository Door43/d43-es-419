====== innocent- inocente ======

===== Definition: =====

**Definición:**

To be innocent means to **not** be guilty of something.

**Ser inocente significa //no// ser culpable de algo.**

   * In the case of a crime, a person is innocent of the crime if he has **not**  committed that crime.
  * **En el caso de un crimen, la persona es inocente si no ha cometido ese crimen**.
  * Jesus is the only human being who was completely innocent, because he never did anything wrong. He was sinless.
  * **Jesus es el único ser humano que fue completamente inocente, porque nunca cometió ningún error, El no tenía pecado**.
The opposite of "innocent" is "guilty."

**Lo contrario de "inocente"  es "culpable"**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:08-06|[08-06]]]** After two years, Joseph was still in prison, even though he was **innocent**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:40-04|[40-04]]]** One of them mocked Jesus, but the other said, "Do you have no fear of God? We are guilty, but this man is **innocent**."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:40-08|[40-08]]]** When the soldier guarding Jesus saw everything that happened, he said, "Certainly, this man was **innocent**. He was the Son of God."
