====== rebel Rebelarse ======

==== (to rebel, rebellious) ====

===== Definition: Definicion: =====

To rebel is to refuse to submit to someone's authority. That is, to refuse to obey one's leader.

**Rebelarse es rehusarse a someter a la autoridad de alguien.  Es decir, rehusarse a obedecer el lider de uno.**

   * Passive rebellion happens when people refuse to do what the authorities over them have commanded.
  * **La rebelion pasiva sucede cuando las gentes rehusan hacer lo que las autoridades que estan sobre ellos les ordenan.**
  * Active rebellion happens when people directly oppose or disobey their authorities.
  * **Rebelion activa sucede cuando las personas directamente se oponen a desobedecer sus autoridades.**
  * To rebel can also refer to people going to war against a government or leader that is ruling over them. People may do this when they believe that the government or leader is ruling unjustly.
  * **Rebelarse puede tambien refereirse a personas que entran en guerra en contra de un gobierno o lider que gobierna sobre ellos.  Las gentes pueden hacer esto cuando creen que el gobierno o lider esta gobernando injustamente.**

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:18-09|[18-09]]]** Jeroboam **rebelled**  against God and caused the people to sin.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:18-13|[18-13]]]** Most of the people of Judah also **rebelled**  against God and worshiped other gods.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:20-07|[20-07]]]** But after a few years, the king of Judah **rebelled**  against Babylon.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:45-03|[45-03]]]** Then he (Stephen) said, "You stubborn and **rebellious**  people always reject the Holy Spirit, just as your ancestors always rejected God and killed his prophets.
