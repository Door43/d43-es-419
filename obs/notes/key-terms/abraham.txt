====== Abraham  Abraham ======

==== (Abraham, Abram) ====

===== Facts: Factores =====

Abraham was the person that God chose to start the family that would grow into the people known as the Israelites.

Abraham fue la persona que Dios escogió para comenzar la familia conocida como los Israelitas.

  * God promised Abraham that he would have many descendants and that they would become a great nation.
  * Dios le prometio a Abraham que tendría muchas descendencia y que ellos serian una gran nación.
  * God made his covenant with Abraham and promised to uphold it with his descendants forever.
  * Dios hizo un pacto con Abraham y prometio mantenerlo con su descendencia por siempre.
  * Abraham believed God and obeyed him.
  * Abraham le creyó a Dios y le obedeció a El.
  * Abraham's name was originally "Abram," but God changed it to Abraham.
  * El nombre original de Abraham era "Abram" pero Dios lo cambio por Abraham.
  * Abram means, "exalted father," and Abraham means, "father coof many."
  * El nombre de Abram significa "padre exaltado", y el nombre Abraham significa  "padre de muchos".

[See also: **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:sarah|Sarah]]**, **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:isaac|Isaac]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:04-06|[04-06]]]** When **Abram**  arrived in Canaan, God said, "Look all around you. I will give to you and your descendants all the land that you can see as an inheritance."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:05-04|[05-04]]]** Then God changed **Abram**'s name to **Abraham**, which means "father of many."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:05-05|[05-05]]]** About a year later, when **Abraham**  was 100 years old and Sarah was 90, Sarah gave birth to Abraham's son.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:05-06|[05-06]]]** When Isaac was a young man, God tested **Abraham's**  faith by saying, "Take Isaac, your only son, and kill him as a sacrifice to me."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:06-01|[06-01]]]** When **Abraham**  was very old and his son, Isaac, had grown to be a man, **Abraham**  sent one of his servants back to the land where his relatives lived to find a wife for his son, Isaac.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:06-04|[06-04]]]** After a long time, **Abraham**  died and all of the promises that God had made to him in the covenant were passed on to Isaac.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:21-02|[21-02]]]** God promised **Abraham**  that through him all people groups of the world would receive a blessing.
