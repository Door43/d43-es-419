====== tempt tentar ======

==== (to tempt, temptation) ====

==== (tentar, tentación) ====

===== Definition: =====

===== Definición: =====

To tempt someone is to try to get that person to do something wrong.

**Tentar a alguien es tratar de forzar a esa persona a que haga algo incorrecto.**

   * A temptation is something that causes a person to want to do something wrong.
  * **Una tentación es algo que provoca a que una persona haga algo incorrecto.**
  * People are tempted by their own sinful nature and by other people.
  * **La gente es tentada por su propia naturaleza pecadora y por otras personas.**
  * Satan also tempts people to disobey God and to sin against God by doing wrong things.
  * **Satán también tenta personas a que desobedezcan a Dios y a que pequen contra Dios haciendo cosas malas.**
  * Jesus resisted all of Satan's temptations. Jesus never sinned.
  * **Jesús resistió todas las tentaciones que Sátanas le puso al frente. Jesús nunca pecó.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:25-01|[25-01]]]** Then Satan came to Jesus and **tempted**  him to sin.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:25-08|[25-08]]]** Jesus did not give in to Satan's **temptations**, so Satan left him.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:38-11|[38-11]]]** Jesus told his disciples to pray that they would not enter into **temptation**.
