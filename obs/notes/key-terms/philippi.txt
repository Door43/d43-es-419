====== Philippi  Filipos ======

===== Facts: =====

**Datos:**

Philippi was a city located in the northern part of ancient Greece.

**Filipos era una ciudad localizada en la parte norte de la antigua Grecia.**

   * Paul and Silas traveled to Philippi to preach about Jesus to the people there.
  * **Pablo y Sila viajaron a Filipos a predicar sobre Jesús a la gente allí.**
  * Paul and Silas were arrested in Philippi, but God miraculously freed them.
  * **Pablo y Silas fueron arrestados en Filipos, pero Dios milagrosamente los libertó.**
  * Paul wrote the letter to the Philippians in the Bible to the Christians in Philippi.
  * **Pablo ** escribió la carta a los FIlipenses de la Biblia a los cristianos en Filipos.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:47-01|[47-01]]]** One day, Paul and his friend Silas went to the town of **Philippi**  to proclaim the good news about Jesus.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:47-13|[47-13]]]** The next day the leaders of the city released Paul and Silas from prison and asked them to leave **Philippi**.
