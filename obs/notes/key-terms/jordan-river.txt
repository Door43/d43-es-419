====== Jordan River **Rio Jordan**  ======

===== Facts: =====

**Datos:**

The Jordan River is a river that flows from north to south, and forms the eastern boundary of the land that was called Canaan.

**El Rio **Jordan es un rio que fluye de norte a sur y forma un limete oriental de la tierra llamada Canaan.** **

   * Today, the Jordan River separates Israel on the west from Jordan on the east.
  * **Hoy, el Rio Jordan separa Israel en la parte oeste del Jordan en el este.**
  * The Jordan River flows through the Sea of Galilee and then empties into the Dead Sea.
  * **El Rio Jordan fluye a traves del Mar de Galilea y se vacia en el Mar Muerto.**
  * When Joshua led the Israelites into Canaan, they had to cross the Jordan River while it was flooding and very deep. Because it was too deep to wade across, God miraculously stopped the water from flowing so they could go across.
  * **Cuando Joshua guio a los Israelitas  al Canaan, ellos tenian que cruzar el Rio Jordan mientras estaba inundado y bien profundo. Porque estaba muy profundo, para caminar a traves, Dios milagrosamente paro el agua de fluir para que ellos pudieran cruzar.**
