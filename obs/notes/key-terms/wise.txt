====== wise  sabio ======

=== (wise, wisdom, wisely)  (sabio, sabiduría, sabiamente) ===

===== Definition:  Definición =====

A person who is "wise" is one who exercises "wisdom." To be wise is to discern or judge a matter rightly.

**Una persona que es "sabio" es aquel que ejercita la "sabiduria".**

   * To have "wisdom" is to possess important knowledge or experience.
  * **Tener sabiduría es poseer conocimiento y experiencia importante.**
  * A "wise" person is able to make good choices.
  * **Una persona sabia está capacitada para tomar buenas decisiones.**
  * To act "wisely" is to know what is right and to do it.
  * **Actiar sabiamente es saber lo que es correcto y hacerlo.**
