====== miracle milagro ======

==== (miracle, wonder, sign) ====

\\
**(milagro, maravilla,senal)**

===== Definition: =====

Miracles are things that could never happen unless God caused them. For example, it was a miracle when God brought a dead person back to life.

**Los milagros son cosas que podrian nunca suceder a menos que Dios los provoque. Por ejemplo, esto fue un milagro cuando Dios trajo una persona muerta de vuelta a la vida. **

   * Miracles are sometimes called "wonders" because people are amazed by them. They are also called "signs" because they show that God is working through them.
  *  \\ **Los milagros algunas veces son llamados "maravillas" porque las personas son asombrados por estos. Estos tambien son llamados "senales" porque ellos muestran que Dios esta trabajando a travez de ellos.**
  * Since miracles are done by the power of God, they are sometimes called, "works of power."
  *  \\ **Debido a que los milagros son realizados por el poder de Dios, ellos son alunas veces llamados, "actos de poder".**
  * People do not have the power to do miracles on their own. However, God has given different people the power to do miracles on many occasions to accomplish his purposes.
  *  \\ **Las personas no tienen el poder para realizar milagros por ellos mismos. Sin embargo, Dios le ha dado poder a diferentes personas para hacer milagros, en muchas ocasiones para cumplir sus propositos.**
  * For example, the prophets asked God to heal people, to provide food from nothing, and even to raise people from the dead, and God did those things.
  *  \\ **Por ejemplo, los profetas le pidieron a Dios sanar personas, proveer comida de la nada, y hasta levantar personas de la muerte, y Dios hizo estas cosas.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:16-08|[16-08]]]** Gideon asked God for two **signs**  so he could be sure that God would use him to save Israel.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-14|[19-14]]]** God did many **miracles**  through Elisha.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:37-10|[37-10]]]** Many of the Jews believed in Jesus because of this **miracle**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:43-06|[43-06]]]**"Men of Israel, Jesus was a man who did many mighty **signs**  and **wonders**  by the power of God, as you have seen and already know."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:49-02|[49-02]]]**] Jesus did many **miracles**  that prove he is God. He walked on water, calmed storms, healed many sick people, drove out demons, raised the dead to life, and turned five loaves of bread and two small fish into enough food for over 5,000 people.
