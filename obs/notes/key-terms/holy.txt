====== holy santo ======

===== Definition: =====

===== Definición: =====

To be holy is to be separated from all sin and evil. Only God is absolutely holy. But he can make things and people holy.

**Ser santo es estar separado de todo pecado y de todo lo que es malo. Solamente Dios es totalmente santo. Dios puede santificar cosas o gente.**

   * A holy person or thing is morally perfect, completely without sin.
  * **Una persona santa o una cosa santa es moralmente perfecto y totalmente libre de pecado.**
  * People and things that God sets apart for a special purpose are called holy, such as a priest who must be ceremonially cleansed of sin in order to serve God, or a place where God reveals himself, such as, "holy ground" or his temple.
  * **Gente y cosas que Dios separa para un propósito especiál son llamadas santas, como un sacerdote que tiene que ser limpiado a travez de una ceremonia para poder ser siervo de Dios, o un lugar  donde Dios se revela al hombre como en una "tierra santa" o en su templo.**
Make sure that the translation of the word "holy" does not mean something that is taboo or forbidden.
