====== Bathsheba  Betsabé     f ======

===== Facts: =====

**Datos: **

Bathsheba was the wife of Uriah, a soldier in King David's army. She became the wife of David, and the mother of Solomon

**Betsabé era la esposa de Urias, un soldado del ejército del Rey David. Ella se convirtió en la esposa de David y la madre de Salomón.**

   * David committed adultery with Bathsheba while she was married to Uriah.
  * **David cometió adulterio con Betsabé mientras ella estaba casada con Urias.**
  * When Bathsheba became pregnant with David's child, David caused Uriah to be killed in battle. David then married Bathsheba.
  * **Cuando Betsabé quedó embarazada con el hijo de David, David causó que mataran a Urias en ****batalla. Entonces David se caso con Betsabé.**
  * God punished David for his sin by causing the child to die several days after he was born.
  * **Dios castigó a David por su pecado causando la muerte de su niño después de varios días de nacido.**
  * Later, Bathsheba gave birth to another son, Solomon, who became king after David.
  * **Después, Betsabé dió a luz otro niño, Salomón, quien se convirtió en rey después de David.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-10|[17-10]]]** One day, when all of David's soldiers were away from home fighting battles, he got up from an afternoon nap and saw a beautiful woman bathing. Her name was **Bathsheba**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-11|[17-11]]]** A short time later **Bathsheba**  sent a message to David saying that she was pregnant.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-12|[17-12]]]****Bathsheba's**  husband, a man named Uriah, was one of David's best soldiers.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** After Uriah was killed, David married **Bathsheba**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-14|[17-14]]]** Later, David and **Bathsheba**  had another son, and they named him Solomon.
