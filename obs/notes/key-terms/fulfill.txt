====== fulfill cumplir ======

===== Definition: =====

To "fulfill" something is to complete or accomplish what was predicted, required, or expected.

**Cumplir algo es completar o lograr lo que fue predecido, requerido o esperado.**

   * When a prophecy is fulfilled, what the prophecy said would happen actually happens.
  * **Cuando una profecia es cumplida, lo que la profecia dice que sucedera, realmente sucede.**
  * Every prophecy that is truly from God will be fulfilled.
  * **Cada una de las profesias de Dios seran cumplidas**.
  * Some prophecies have already been partially fulfilled, but will be completely fulfilled at a later time.
  * **Algunas profecias han sido parcialmente cumplidas, pero seran completadas totalmente mas adelante.**
  * Many of the prophecies about the Messiah were fulfilled hundreds of years after they were spoken by the prophets. Some of the events that were prophesied have not yet happened, but they will be fulfilled when Jesus returns.
  * **Muchas de las profecias acerca de el Mesias fueron cumplidas cientos de anos despues de que estas fueron dichas por los profetas. Algunas de los eventos que fueron profetizados aun no han sucedido, pero estas se cumpliran cuando Jesus regrese.**
  * "Fulfill" can be translated as, "accomplish" or, "make happen."
  * **"cumplir" puede ser trasladado como "lograr" o "hacer que suceda"**
[See also: **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:prophet|prophecy]]**, **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:messiah|Messiah]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:24-04|[24-04]]]** John **fulfilled**  what the prophets said, "See I send my messenger ahead of you, who will prepare your way."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:40-03|[40-03]]]** The soldiers gambled for Jesus' clothing. When they did this, they **fulfilled**  a prophecy that said, "They divided my garments among them, and gambled for my clothing."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:42-07|[42-07]]]** Jesus said, "I told you that everything written about me in God's word must be **fulfilled**."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:43-05|[43-05]]]**"This **fulfills**  the prophecy made by the prophet Joel in which God said, 'In the last days, I will pour out my Spirit.'"
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:43-07|[43-07]]]**"This **fulfills**  the prophecy which says, 'You will not let your Holy One rot in the grave.'"
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:44-05|[44-05]]]**"Although you did not understand what you were doing, God used your actions to **fulfill**  the prophecies that the Messiah would suffer and die."
