====== forgive perdonar ======

==== (to forgive, forgiveness) ====

**(perdonar, perdón)**

===== Definition: =====

**Definici****ón****:**

To forgive someone means to not resent that person and not be angry at him for doing something hurtful.

**El perdonar a alguien significa que uno ya no tiene resentimiento contra esa persona y no est<font 14px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>á</font> enojado con él por haberlo lastimado.**
  * Forgiving someone often means not punishing that person for something he has done wrong.
  * **Perdonar a alguien significa no castigar a la persona por el error cometido.**
  * This can also mean not making someone pay back money he owes, as in "forgiving a debt."
  * **Esto también significa que uno no demanda que le devuelvan dinero; por ejemplo, como****en "perdonar una deuda"**
  * Forgiveness is the act of forgiving.
  * **Perdonar es el acto de olvidar**.
  * When people confess their sins, God forgives them based on Jesus' sacrificial death on the cross.
  * **Cuando las personas confiesan sus pecados, Dios los perdona, basado en el sacrificio de la cruz.**
  * Jesus taught his disciples to forgive others as he has forgiven them.
  * **Jesus enseñó a sus discípulos a perdonar a otros, como El los perdonó a ellos. **
