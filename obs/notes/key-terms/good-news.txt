====== good news ======

Las buenas nuevas

===== Definition: =====

Definicion:

The phrase, "good news" means a message or announcement that tells people something that benefits them and makes them glad.

la frase: "Buenas Nuevas" significa el mensaje o anuncio que dice a la gente algo que les beneficiara y les hara felices.

  * In the Bible, the phrase "the good news" often refers specifically to the message that God sent his Son to earth to save people from their sins: God forgives all people who trust in his Son, and he gives them eternal life.
  * En la Bilbia, la frase "buenas nuevas" se refiere especificamente al mensaje de que Dios envio a su hijo al mundo para salvar a su pueblo de sus pecados: Dios perdona a toda la gente que cree en su Hijo, y que El les da vida eterna.
  * This could also be translated as, "good message" or, "good announcement" or, "glad word."
  * Esto tambien puede ser traducido como: "un buen mensaje" o "un buen anuncio", o "una palabra amable."
  * The "good news" is also called the "good news of Jesus," the "good news about Jesus," and the "good news of the kingdom."
  * Las "buenas nuevas" tambien pueden llamarse "las buenas nuevas de Jesus", y tambien: "las buenas nuevas del reino."
  * In many Christian contexts, the good news is called "the gospel."
  * En muchos contextos Cristianos, las "buenas nuevas" son llamadas: "el evangelio."
