God God

==== (god, gods) ====

===== Definition: =====

Definicion:

A god is something that people worship because they believe it has great power to affect their lives.

Un dios es algo que la gente adora porque creen que posee un gran poder que puede afectar sus vidas.

  * When god is written with a small 'g,' it refers to a false god.
  * Cuando la palabra "dios" se escribe con "g" minuscula, se refiere a un dios falso.
  * When God is written with a capital 'G,' it refers to the one and only true God.
  * Cuando la palabra Dios se escribe con "G" mayuscula, se refiere al unico y verdadero Dios.
  * These "gods" do not exist. Yahweh is the only God, and he has no rival.
  * Estos "dioses" no existen. Yahweh es el unico Dios y no tiene rivales.
  * People sometimes make idols as symbols of their false gods.
  * A veces la gente hace idolos como simbolos de sus falsos dioses.
  * Demons often deceive people into believing that these false gods and their idols have power.
  * Los demonios enganan continuamente a la gente haciendoles creer que estos dioses falsos y sus idolos tienen algun poder.
