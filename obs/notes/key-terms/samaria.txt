====== Samaria  Samaria ======

==== (Samaria, Samaritan) ====

**(Samaria, Samaritanos)**

===== Facts: =====

**Datos**:

Samaria was a region in the northern part of Israel.

**Samaria era una región en la parte norte de Israel.**

   * When the Assyrians conquered the kingdom of Israel, they took most of the people away to Assyria and brought foreigners to Israel to live in that area.
  * **Cuando los asirios conquistaron el reino de Israel, ellos sacaron a casi toda las personas de alli a Asiria y trajeron extranjeros a vivir en aquella área.**
  * Some of the Israelites married the foreigners, and their descendants were called Samaritans.
  * **Algunos de los israelitas se casaron con extranjeras y sus descendientes fueron llamados Samaritanos.**
  * The Samaritans worshiped other gods besides Yahweh.
  * **Los Samaritanos rendían culto a otros dioses fuera de Jehova.**
  * The Jews and the Samaritans hated each other.
  * **Los judíos y los samaritanos se odiaban entre sí.**
