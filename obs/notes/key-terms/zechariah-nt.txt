====== Zechariah Zacarías ======

===== Facts: Hechos =====

Zechariah was a Jewish priest who became the father of John the Baptist.

**Zacarias era un sacerdote judio, padre de Juan el Bautista.**

   * Zechariah loved God and obeyed him.
  * **Zacarías amaba a Dios y le obedecía.**
  * For many years Zechariah and his wife, Elizabeth, prayed earnestly to have a child, but did not have one. Then when they were very old, God answered their prayers and gave them a son.
  * **Por muchos años Zacarías y su esposa, Elisabet, oraron sinceramente para tener un hijo, pero no tenían ninguno.  Entonces cuando fueron viejos,  Dios respondio sus oraciones y les dió un hijo.**
  * Zechariah prophesied that his son John would be the prophet who would announce and prepare the way for the Messiah.
  * **Zacarías profetizó que su hijo Juan sería el profeta que anunciaría y prepararía el camino del Mesias.**
