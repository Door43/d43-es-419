====== Midian (Madian, Madianitas) ======

==== (Midian, Midianites) ====

===== Facts: =====

Midian was a people group located in the northern Arabian Desert to the south of the land of Canaan. The people of that group were called "Midianites."

\\
**Madian era un pueblo localizado al norte de el Desierto de Arabia y al Sur de la tierra de Canaan. Las personas de este grupo fueron llamados "Madianitas".**

   * Midian existed from around the 19th to the 11th centuries B.C.
  * **Madian existe ****alrededor**** de los siglos IXX al XI A.C**.
  * Joseph was taken to Egypt by a group of Midianite slave traders.
  * **Jose fue llevado a Egipto por un grupo de Madianitas vendedores de esclavos.**
  *
  * Many years later the Midianites attacked and raided the Israelites in the land of Canaan. Gideon led the Israelites in defeating them.
  * **Muchos anos despues los Madianitas atacaron y asaltaron a los Israelitas en la tierra de Canaan. Gedeon lidereo a los Israelitas a derrotarlos.**
  * Many of the modern-day Arabian tribes are descendants of this group.
  *  \\ **Muchos de los tribus Arabes de los modernos dias, descienden de este grupo.**
