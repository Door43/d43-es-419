====== Jacob **Jacob**  ======

===== Facts: =====

**Jacob**

Jacob was the second of the twin sons that were born to Isaac and Rebekah. The first twin born was Esau.

**Jacob era el segundo de los gemelos que nacieron de Isaac y Rebeca. El primero que nacio fue Esau.**

   * Jacob's name means "deceiver" or "trickster."
  * **El nombre de Jacob significa "enganador" o "embaucador"**
  * Jacob was clever and deceptive. He found ways to take both the firstborn inheritance rights and the blessing from his older brother, Esau.
  * **Jacob era inteligente y enganoso. El encontro formas para quitarle ambos, los derechos de nacimiento y la bendicion a su hermano mayor Esau.**
  * Esau was angry and planned to kill him, so Jacob left his homeland. But he later returned and lived peacefully with his brother.
  * **Esau tenia mucho coraje y planiificomatarlo, pero Jacob se fue de su patria. Pero el despues regreso y en paz con su hermano.**
  * God changes Jacob's name to Israel, which means, "He struggles with God."
  * **Dios cambio el nombre de Jacob a Israel, que significa, "El lucho con Dios"**
  * God kept his covenant to Abraham and his descendants through Abraham's son, Isaac, and Isaac's son, Jacob.
  * **Dios guardo el pacto con Abrahan y sus descendientes a traves del hijo de Abrahan Isaac, Jacob.**
  * Jacob had twelve sons. Their descendants became the twelve tribes of Israel.
  * **Jacob tuvo 12 hijos. Sus descendientes se convirtieron en las doce tribus de Israel.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:07-01|[07-01]]]** As the boys grew up, Rebekah loved **Jacob**, but Isaac loved Esau. **Jacob** loved to stay at home, but Esau loved to hunt.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:07-07|[07-07]]]**  **Jacob** lived there for many years, and during that time he married and had twelve sons and a daughter. God made him very wealthy.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:07-08|[07-08]]]** After twenty years away from his home in Canaan, **Jacob** returned there with his family, his servants, and all his herds of animals.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:07-10|[07-10]]]** The covenant promises God had promised to Abraham and then to Isaac now passed on to **Jacob**.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:08-01|[08-01]]]** Many years later, when **Jacob** was an old man, he sent his favorite son, Joseph, to check on his brothers who were taking care of the herds.
