====== Rebekah Rebeca ======

===== Facts: Verdades: =====

Rebekah was a daughter of one of Abraham's nephews. Her grandfather was Abraham's brother.

**Rebeca era una hija de uno de los sobrinos de Abraham.  Su abuelo fue hermano de Abraham.**

   * God chose Rebekah to be the wife of Abraham's son Isaac.
  * **Dios escogio a Rebeca para ser la esposa del hijo de Abraham Isaac.**
  * Rebekah left her country and went with Abraham's servant to go to Isaac's country, marry Isaac, and live there with his family
  * **Rebeca dejo a su pais y se fue con el siervo de Abraham para ir al pais de Isaac, casarse con Isaac, y vivio alli con su familia.**
  * For a long time Rebekah did not have any children, but finally God blessed her with twin boys, Esau and Jacob.
  * **Por mucho tiempo Rebeca no tuvo ningun hijo, pero finalmente Dios la bendijo con mellizos, Esau y Jacob**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:06-02|[06-02]]]** After a very long journey to the land where Abraham's relatives lived, God led the servant to **Rebekah**. She was the granddaughter of Abraham's brother.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:06-06|[06-06]]]** God told **Rebekah**, "There are two nations inside of you."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:07-01|[07-01]]]** As the boys grew up, **Rebekah**  loved Jacob, but Isaac loved Esau.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:07-03|[07-03]]]** Isaac wanted to give his blessing to Esau. But before he did, **Rebekah**  and Jacob tricked him by having Jacob pretend to be Esau.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:07-06|[07-06]]]** But **Rebekah**  heard of Esau's plan. So she sent Jacob far away to live with her relatives.
