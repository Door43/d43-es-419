====== Tent of Meeting La Tienda de Reunión ======

===== Facts: =====

===== Datos: =====

When God gave his laws at Mount Sinai, he told the Israelites to make the Tent of Meeting. This was the holy place where he would meet with them. As they wandered through the wilderness, they would take it with them and set it up whenever God told them to do so.

**Cuando Dios dió sus leyes en el Monte Sinaí, el le dijo a los israelitas que construyeran una tienda de reunión. Este seria el lugar santo donde el se encontraría con ellos. Cuando andaban en su peregrinaje, ellos llevaban la Tienda de Reunión con ellos y la montaban dondequiera que Dios les decía. **

   * The Tent of Meeting was like a house made of cloth stretched over a wooden framework.
  * **La Tienda de Reunión era como una casa hecha de ****telón**** que estaba estirada sobre una infraestructura de madera.**
  * God told the people to set up the Tent of Meeting in the middle of a large courtyard surrounded by walls made of cloth and wood.
  * **Dios le dijo a la gente que montaran una Tienda de Reunión en el medio de un gran patio rodeado por paredes hechas de**** telón**** y de madera.**
  * God told them to put the altar in front of the Tent of Meeting in the middle of the courtyard. The people could go into the courtyard to give offerings and sacrifices to God. The priests who were on duty would receive what the people brought and then offer it on the altar for a sacrifice as the law required.
  * **Dios les dijo que posicionaran el altar al frente de la Tienda de Reunión en el medio del patio. La gente podía entrar al patio a dar ofrendas y sacrificios a Dios. Los sacerdotes que estaban laborando, recibían lo que la gente traía y después lo ofrecían en otro altar como un sacrificio que era requerido por la ley.**
  * The Tent of Meeting had a thick curtain hanging in the middle of it, which divided it into two rooms. The curtain was used as barrier to keep people from seeing the room on the other side, and to keep people from going into it. The first room was very holy, so only the priests could go into it. The second room was the most holy place, so only the high priest could go into it.
  * **La Tienda de Reunión tenía una cortina gruesa que colgaba en el centro y la dividía en dos cuartos. La cortina era usada como una pared para evitar que la gente viera el cuarto adentro y para que no entraran. El primer cuarto era santo y solamente los sacerdotes podían entrar. El segundo cuarto era el lugar ****santísimo y solamente el sumo sacerdote podía entrar.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:13-08|[13-08]]]** God gave the Israelites a detailed description of a tent he wanted them to make. It was called the **Tent of Meeting**, and it had two rooms, separated by a large curtain.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:13-09|[13-09]]]** Anyone who disobeyed God's law could bring an animal to the altar in front of the **Tent of Meeting**  as a sacrifice to God.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:14-08|[14-08]]]** God was very angry and came to the **Tent of Meeting**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:18-02|[18-02]]]** Instead of at the **Tent of Meeting**, people now worshiped God and offered sacrifices to him at the Temple.
