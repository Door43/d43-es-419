====== fast __//ayunar//__  ======

===== Definition: =====

Definición:

To fast is to stop eating food, or certain foods and drinks, for a period of time, such as for a day or more.

**Ayunar es abstenerse de comer completamente, o ciertas alimentos y bebidas, por un periodo de tiempo, como uno o más dias.**

   * Fasting can help people to focus on God and pray without being distracted by food.
  * **El ayunar puede ayudar a las personas a enfocarse en Dios y en la oración, sin ser distraídos por la comida.**
  * Jesus condemned the Jewish religious leaders for fasting for the wrong reasons. They fasted so that others would think they were very religious people.
  * **Jesús condenó los líderes religiosos judíos por ayunar por las razones equivocadas. Ellos ayunaban para que otros pensaran que ellos eran muy religiosos.**
  * Sometimes people fast in order to show that they are very sad or grieved about something.
  * **Algunas veces las personas ayunan para mostrar que ellos estan muy tristes o afligidos por algo.**
[See also: **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:jewish-leaders|Jewish leaders]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:25-01|[25-01]]]** Immediately after Jesus was baptized, the Holy Spirit led him out into the wilderness, where he **fasted**  for forty days and forty nights.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:34-08|[34-08]]]**"'For example, I **fast**  two times every week and I give you ten percent of all the money and goods that I receive.'"
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:46-10|[46-10]]]** One day, while the Christians at Antioch were **fasting**  and praying, the Holy Spirit said to them, "Set apart for me Barnabas and Saul to do the work I have called them to do."
