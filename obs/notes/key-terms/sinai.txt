====== Sinai  Sinaí ======

==== (Sinai, Mount Sinai) ====

(Sinaí, Monte Sinaí)

===== Facts: =====

Datos:

Sinai is the name of a mountain that was probably located in the southern part of what is now called the Sinai Peninsula.

**Sinaí es el nombre de la montaña que estaba probablemente localizada en la parte sureste de lo que hoy se conoce como la Península de Sinaí.**

   * The Israelites came to Mount Sinai as they were traveling from Egypt to the Promised Land.
  * **Los israelitas fueron al Monte Sinaí cuando viajaban de Egipto a la Tierra Prometida.**
  * Mount Sinai is part of a large, rocky desert.
  * **El Monte Sinaí es parte de un gran desierto rocoso.**
  * God gave Moses the Ten Commandments on Mount Sinai.
  * **Dios dió a  Moisés los Diez Mandamientos en el Monte Sinaí.**
[See also: **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:promised-land|Promised Land]]**, **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:ten-commandments|Ten Commandments]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:13-01|[13-01]]]** After God led the Israelites through the Red Sea, he led them through the wilderness to a mountain called **Sinai**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:13-03|[13-03]]]** Three days later, after the people had prepared themselves spiritually, God came down on top of **Mount Sinai**  with thunder, lightning, smoke, and a loud trumpet blast.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:13-11|[13-11]]]** For many days, Moses was on top of **Mount Sinai**  talking with God.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:15-13|[15-13]]]** Then Joshua reminded the people of their obligation to obey the covenant that God had made with the Israelites at **Sinai**.
