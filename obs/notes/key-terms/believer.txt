====== believer  creyente     f ======

===== Definition: =====

**Definición:**

A "believer" is a person who trusts completely in a person or an idea.

**Un "creyente" es una persona que cree completamente en otra persona o una idea.**

   * In the Bible, "believer" specifically refers to someone who believes in Jesus.
  * **En la Biblia, "creyente" se refiere específicamente a alguien que cree en Jesús.**
  * The term "Christian" eventually came to be the main title for believers because it indicates that they believe in Christ.
  * **El término "Cristiano" eventualmente vino a ser el nombre dado a los creyentes, porque este indica que ellos creían en Cristo.**
  * Other common ways to translate "believer" are, "follower of Jesus" or, "disciple."
  * **Otras formas comunes de traducir "creyente" son "seguidor de Jesús" o "discípulo".**
[See also: **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:believe|believe]]**, **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:christian|Christian]]**, **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:disciple|disciple]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:43-01|[43-01]]]** After Jesus returned to heaven, the disciples stayed in Jerusalem as Jesus had commanded them to do. The **believers**  there constantly gathered together to pray.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:43-03|[43-03]]]** While the **believers**  were all together, suddenly the house where they were was filled with a sound like a strong wind. Then something that looked like flames of fire appeared over the heads of all the **believers**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:43-13|[43-13]]]** Every day, more people became **believers**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:46-06|[45-06]]]** That day many people in Jerusalem started persecuting the followers of Jesus, so the **believers**  fled to other places. But in spite of this, they preached about Jesus everywhere they went.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:46-01|[46-01]]]** Saul was the young man who guarded the robes of the men who killed Stephen. He did not believe in Jesus, so he persecuted the **believers**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:46-09|[46-09]]]** Some **believers**  who fled from the persecution in Jerusalem went far away to the city of Antioch and preached about Jesus…It was at Antioch that **believers**  in Jesus were first called "Christians."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:47-14|[47-14]]]** They also wrote many letters to encourage and teach the **believers**  in the churches.
