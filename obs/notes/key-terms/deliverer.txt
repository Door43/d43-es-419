====== deliverer ======

salvador / rescatador / libertador

===== Definition: =====

Definicion:

A deliverer is someone who rescues people or sets people free.

Un salvador es alguien que rescata a alguien, o que libera a alguien sin pedir recompensa.

  * The word "deliverer" can also be translated as, "rescuer."
  * La palabra "salvador" tambien puede ser traducida como "rescatador o libertador."
  * The deliverers in Israel were leaders who rescued the Israelite people from their enemies by leading them in battle against their enemies.
  * Los libertadores en Israel eran lideres que rescataban a la gente de sus enemigos, guiandolos en batallas contra sus enemigos.
  * In many cases, the deliverer functioned as a military leader.
  * En muchos casos, el libertador funcionaba como un lider militar.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:16-03|[16-03]]]** Then God provided a **deliverer**  who rescued them from their enemies and brought peace to the land.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:16-16|[16-16]]]** They (Israel) finally asked God for help again, and God sent them another **deliverer**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:16-17|[16-17]]]** Over many years, God sent many **deliverers**  who saved the Israelites from their enemies.
