====== Esau Esaú B ======

===== Facts: =====

**Datos:**

Esau was one of the twin sons of Isaac and Rebekah. He was the first baby born. His twin brother was Jacob.

**Esaú era uno de los gemelos de Isaac y Rebeca. El fue el primer bebé en nacer. Su gemelo era Jacob.**

   * Esau sold his birthright to his brother Jacob in exchange for a bowl of food.
  * **Esaú vendió su primogenitura a su hermano Jacob a cambio de un plato de comida.**
  * Since Esau was born first, his father was supposed to give him a special blessing. But Jacob stole Esau's blessing. At first Esau was so angry that he wanted to kill Jacob.
  * **Como Esaú fue el primero en nacer, se suponía que su papá le diera una bendición especial. Pero Jacob robó la bendición de Esau. A principio, Esaú ****ten****í****​****a tanto coraje que quería matar a Jacob.**
  * Esau had many children and grandchildren, and these descendants formed a large people group living in the land of Canaan.
  * **Esaú tenía muchos hijos y nietos, y estos decendientes formaron un grupo grande de gente viviendo en la tierra de Caanaan.**
