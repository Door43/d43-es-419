====== Ten Commandments Los Diez Mandamientos ======

===== Facts: =====

===== Datos: =====

God told Moses many commands that He wanted the Israelites to obey. He wrote ten of these commands on two slabs of stone.

**Dios le dió a Moisés muchos mandamientos que El quería que los israelitas obedecieran. Moisés escribió diez de esos mandamientos en dos tablas de piedra.**

   * These Ten Commandments were special commands to help the Israelites love and worship God and love other people.
  * **Estos Diez Mandamientos eran mandamientos especiales para que los israelitas pudieran amar y alabar a Dios y amar a otra gente.**
  * These commandments were also part of God's covenant with his people. By obeying what God had commanded them to do, the people of Israel would show that they loved God and belonged to him.
  * **Estos mandamientos también eran parte del pacto que Dios tenía con su pueblo. Al obedecer lo que Dios había ordenado a hacer, el pueblo de Israel mostraría que ellos amaban a Dios y que le pertenecían a El.**

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:13-07|[13-07]]]** Then God wrote these **Ten Commandments**  on two stone tablets and gave them to Moses.

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:13-15|[13-15]]]** Moses wrote the **Ten Commandments**  on new stone tablets to replace the ones he had broken.
