====== chariot //__Carruaje__ //f ======

Definition:

<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Definición</font>

In ancient times, chariots were lightweight carts with two wheels that were pulled by horses.

<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** En tiempos antiguos, los carruajes eran carretas livianas con dos ruedas haladas por caballos.  **</font>

  * People would sit or stand in chariots, using them for war or travel.
  * **La gente podia sentarse o permanecer de pie en esos carruajes, y los usaban para la guerra o como una forma de transporte.**
  * In war, an army that had chariots had a great advantage of speed and mobility over an army that did not have chariots.
  * **En la guerra, un ejercito con carruajes tenia una gran ventaja en velocidad y mobilidad sobre aquellos ejércitos que nos los tenían.**
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Examples from the Bible stories:</font>

</font>

  * **[12-10]]] **  So they followed the Israelites onto the path through the sea, but God caused the Egyptians to panic and caused their ** chariots **  to get stuck.</font>
  * **[45-07]]] **  As he walked along the road, Philip saw an important official from Ethiopia riding in his ** chariot **  .</font>
