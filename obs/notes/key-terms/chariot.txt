====== <font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Carruaje</font>  ======

===== <font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Definition:</font>  =====

<font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Definicion</font>

<font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In ancient times, chariots were lightweight carts with two wheels that were pulled by horses.</font>

<font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>En tiempos antiguos, los carruajes eran carretas livianas con dos ruedas jaladas por caballos.</font>

  * <font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>People would sit or stand in chariots, using them for war or travel.</font>
  * <font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>La gente podia sentarse o permanecer de pie en esos carruajes, y las usaban para la guerra o como una forma de transporte.</font>
  * <font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In war, an army that had chariots had a great advantage of speed and mobility over an army that did not have chariots.</font>
  * <font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>En la guerra, un ejercito con carruajes tenia una gran ventaja en velocidad y mobilidad que aquellos ejercitos que nos los tenian.</font>
