====== Joseph (NT) **Jose (NT)**  ======

===== Facts: =====

**Datos:**

Joseph was Jesus' earthly father and raised him as his son.

**Jose era el padre terrenal  de Jesus y lo crio al el como su hijo.**

   * Joseph was a descendant of King David.
  * **Jose era descendiente del Rey David.**
  * Joseph was engaged to a virgin girl named Mary, whom God had chosen to be the mother of Jesus the Messiah.
  * **Jose estaba comprometido con una nina virgen llamada Maria, que fue ****e**scogida por Dios para** ser la madre de Jesus el**  Mesias.
  * An angel told Joseph that the Holy Spirit had miraculously made Mary pregnant, and that Mary's baby was the Son of God.
  * **Un angel le dijo a Jose que el Espiritu Santo habia milagrosamente prenado a Maria, y que el bebe de Maria era el Hijo de Dios.**
  * Joseph believed the angel and obeyed God by taking Mary as his wife.
  * **Jose creyo al angel y obedecio a Dios aceptando a Maria como su esposa.**
  * Joseph kept Mary a virgin until after Jesus was born.
  * **Jose guardo a Maria una virgen hasta que Jesus nacio.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:22-04|[22-04]]]** She (Mary) was a virgin and was engaged to be married to a man named **Joseph**.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:23-01|[23-01]]]** **Joseph**, the man Mary was engaged to, was a righteous man. When he heard that Mary was pregnant, he knew it was not his baby. He did not want to shame her, so he planned to quietly divorce her.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:23-02|[23-02]]]** The angel said, "**Joseph**, do not be afraid to take Mary as your wife. The baby in her body is from the Holy Spirit. She will give birth to a son. Name him Jesus (which means, 'Yahweh saves'), because he will save the people from their sins."
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:23-03|[23-03]]]** So **Joseph** married Mary and took her home as his wife, but he did not sleep with her until she had given birth.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:23-04|[23-04]]]** **Joseph** and Mary had to make a long journey from where they lived in Nazareth to Bethlehem because their ancestor was David whose hometown was Bethlehem.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:26-04|[26-04]]]** Jesus said, "The words I just read to you are happening right now." All the people were amazed. "Isn't this the son of **Joseph**?" they said.
