====== Rahab Rahab ======

===== Facts: Verdades: =====

Rahab was a woman who lived in Jericho when Israel attacked the city. She was a prostitute.

**Rahab fue una mujer que vivio en Jerico cuando Israel ataco la ciudad.  Ella fue una prostituta**

   * Rahab hid the two Israelites who came to spy on Jericho before the Israelites attacked it. She helped the spies escape back to the Israelite camp.
  * **Rahab escocndio a dos Israelitas que vinieron a espiar en Jerico antes que los Israelitas la atacaran.  Ella ayudo a los espias escapar de vuelta a los predios Israelitas.**
  * Rahab became a believer in Yahweh and she and her family were saved when Jericho was destroyed. They came to live with the Israelites.
  * **Rahab se hizo una creyente en Yahweh y ella y su familia se salvaron cuando Jerico fue destruida.  Ellos vinieron a vivir con los Israelitas.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:15-01|[15-01]]]** In that city there lived a prostitute named **Rahab**  who hid the spies and later helped them to escape. She did this because she believed God. They promised to protect **Rahab**  and her family when the Israelites would destroy Jericho.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:15-05|[15-05]]]** The Israelites destroyed everything in the city as God had commanded. **Rahab**  and her family were the only people in the city that they did not kill. They became part of the Israelites.
