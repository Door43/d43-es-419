====== Elijah **Elias** ======

===== Facts: =====

**Datos:**

Elijah was one of the most important prophets of Yahweh. Elijah prophesied when Ahab was the king of the northern kingdom of Israel.

**Elias era uno de los profetas mas importantes de Jehová. Elías profesaba cuando Ahab al norte era el rey del reino de Israel.**

   * Elijah confronted Ahab because Ahab encouraged the people to worship false gods.
  * **Elias confronto a Ahab porque Ahab alento a la gente para alabar a falsos dioses.**
  * God provided food and shelter for Elijah in miraculous ways.
  * **Dios proveo comida y abrigo a Elias en formas milagrosas.**
  * God did many miracles through Elijah and showed Israel that Yahweh is the only true God.
  * **Dios hizo muchos milagros a traves de Elias para ensenarles a Israel que Jesus es el unico verdadero Dios.**
  * Elijah confronted the 450 prophets of Baal and demonstrated that Baal is a false god. As a result, the people in the kingdom of Israel killed the prophets of Baal and temporarily worshiped only Yahweh.
  * **Elias confronto a los 450 profetas de Baal y demostro que Baal era un dios falso. Como resultado, la gente del reino de Israel mato a los profetas de Baal y alabaron a Jesus temporadamente.**
  * Elijah did not die–he was taken up to heaven alive. Elijah and Moses then appeared and talked with Jesus hundreds of years later when Jesus was getting ready to die in Jerusalem.
  * **Elias no murio-el fue llevado al cielo con vida. Elias y Moses se aparecieron y ablaron con Jesus sientos de anos despues cuando Jesus se estaba preparando para morir en Jerusalen.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-02|[19-02]]]****Elijah**  was a prophet when Ahab was king over the kingdom of Israel.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-02|[19-02]]]****Elijah**  said to Ahab, "There will be no rain or dew in the kingdom of Israel until I say so."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-03|[19-03]]]** God told **Elijah**  to go to a stream in the wilderness to hide from Ahab who wanted to kill him. Every morning and every evening, birds would bring him bread and meat.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-04|[19-04]]]** But they took care of **Elijah**, and God provided for them so that their flour jar and their bottle of oil never became empty.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-05|[19-05]]]** After three and a half years, God told **Elijah**  to return to the kingdom of Israel and speak with Ahab because he was going to send rain again.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-07|[19-07]]]** Then **Elijah**  said to the prophets of Baal, "Kill a bull and prepare it as a sacrifice, but do not light the fire."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-12|[19-12]]]** Then **Elijah**  said, "Do not let any of the prophets of Baal escape!"
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:36-03|[36-03]]]** Then Moses and the prophet **Elijah**  appeared. These men had lived hundreds of years before this. They talked with Jesus about his death that would soon happen in Jerusalem.
