====== believe crer ======

=== (believe, believe in) (crer, crer en) ===

===== Definition: Definiacion: =====

"Believe" can have two related but different senses:

"crer" **puede tener dos relasonado pero differente sinificados: **
=== 1. Believe crer ===

  * To believe something is to accept or trust that it is true.
  * **Crer en algo es acceptar o confiar que es verda.**
  * To believe someone is to acknowledge that what that person has said is true.
  * ** Crer en algien es amitir que lo que esa persona dijo es verda. **
=== 2. Believe in crer en ===

  * To believe in someone is to trust that that person is reliable. In other words, it is to trust that the person is who he says he is, that he always speaks the truth, and that he will do what he has promised to do.
  * **Crer en algien es confiar que esa persona es segura. En otra palabras, es confiar que la persona es quien dise que es, que el siempre habla la verda, y que el va hacer lo que prometio hacer.**
  * To believe in an idea or principle is to be sure that that idea or principle is the right way to act or live. For example, a person might believe in honesty, and therefore always tell the truth.
  * Crer en una idea o principio es estar segura que esa idea o principio es el correcto modo de actuar o vivir. Por enjemplo, una persona puede crer en honestia, y siempre decir la verda.
  * When a person truly believes in something, the person will act in such a way that shows that belief.
  * Quando una persona en verda crer en algo, la persona va actuar en un modo que ensenia esa creensia.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:03-04|[03-04]]]** Noah warned the people about the coming flood and told them to turn to God, but they did not **believe**  him.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:04-08|[04-08]]]** Abram **believed**  God's promise. God declared that Abram was righteous because he **believed**  God's promise.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:11-02|[11-02]]]** God provided a way to save the firstborn of anyone who **believed in**  him.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:11-06|[11-06]]]** But the Egyptians did not **believe**  God or obey his commands.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:37-05|[37-05]]]** Jesus replied, "I am the Resurrection and the Life. Whoever **believes in**  me will live, even though he dies. Everyone who **believes in**  me will never die. Do you **believe**  this?"
