====== suffer sufrir ======

==== (to suffer, suffering) ====

**(sufrir, sufriendo)**
===== Definition: =====

Definicion:

To suffer is to experience something very unpleasant, such as illness, pain, or great hardship.

**Sufrir es experimentar algo que no es placentero, como una enfermedad, dolor o una gran pena.**

   * Suffering is often necessary to accomplish a specific purpose.
  * **El sufrimiento es a veces necesario para alcanzar un propósito específico.**
  * Some suffering is involuntary–it happens to us outside of our control.
  * **Algunos****sufrimientos son involuntarios-nos suceden por causas fuera de nuestro control.**
  * People can also choose to suffer for a reason, such as when the Messiah decided to suffer in order to pay for our sins.
  * **Las personas también pueden elegir sufrir por alguna razón, como cuando el Mesias decidió sufrir para pagar así por nuestros pecados.**

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:42-03|[42-03]]]** He (Jesus) reminded them that the prophets said the Messiah would **suffer**  and be killed, but would rise again on the third day.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:42-07|[42-07]]]** He (Jesus) said, "It was written long ago that the Messiah would **suffer**, die, and rise from the dead on the third day."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:44-05|[44-05]]]**"Although you did not understand what you were doing, God used your actions to fulfill the prophecies that the Messiah would **suffer**  and die."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:46-04|[46-04]]]** God said, "I have chosen him (Saul) to declare my name to the unsaved. I will show him how much he must **suffer**  for my sake."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:50-17|[50-17]]]** He (Jesus) will wipe away every tear and there will be no more **suffering**, sadness, crying, evil, pain, or death.
