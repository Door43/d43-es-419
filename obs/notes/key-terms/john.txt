====== John (the apostle) **Juan (el apostol)**  ======

===== Facts: =====

**Datos:**

John was one of Jesus' twelve apostles and one of Jesus' closest friends.

**Juan era uno de los doce apostoles de Jesus y uno de los amigos cercano a Jesus.**

   * He was a different person than John the Baptist.
  * **El era una persona diferente que Juan el Bautista.**
  * After Jesus went back to heaven, John (the apostle) taught people about Jesus.
  * **Despues que Jesus fue para atras al cielo, Juan (el apostol) enseno sobre Jesus.**
  * John also wrote two books and some letters about Jesus that are included in the Bible.
  * **Juan tambien escribio dos libros y algunas cartas sobre Jesus que estan incluidas en la Biblia.**
[See also: **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:john-baptist|John (the Baptist)]]**, **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:apostle|apostle]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:36-01|[36-01]]]** One day, Jesus took three of his disciples, Peter, James, and **John** with him. (The disciple named **John** was not the same person who baptized Jesus.) They went up on a high mountain by themselves.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:44-01|[44-01]]]** One day, Peter and **John** were going to the Temple. As they approached the Temple gate, they saw a crippled man who was begging for money. 
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:44-06|[44-06]]]** The leaders of the Temple were very upset by what Peter and **John** were saying. So they arrested them and put them into prison.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:44-07|[44-07]]]** The next day, the Jewish leaders brought Peter and **John** to the high priest and the other religious leaders. They asked Peter and **John**, "By what power did you heal this crippled man?" 
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:44-09|[44-09]]]** The leaders were shocked that Peter and **John** spoke so boldly because they could see that these men were ordinary men who were uneducated. But then they remembered that these men had been with Jesus. After they threatened Peter and **John**, they let them go.
