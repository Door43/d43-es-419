====== Jewish leaders **Lideres Judios**  ======

==== (Jewish leaders, religious leaders) ====


===== Facts: =====

**Datos:**

The Jewish leaders were religious leaders, such as priests and experts in God's laws.

**Los lideres Judios era lideres religiosos, tal como los sacerdotes y expertos en las leyes de Dios.**

   * Many of the religious leaders did not believe that Jesus was the Messiah and Son of God. They were jealous of Jesus and did not want other people to believe in him either.
  * **Muchos de los lideres relgiosos no creian que Jesus era el Mesias y Hijo de Dios. Ellos tenian envidia de Jesus y no querian que otra gente creyeran en el tampoco.**
  * Some of the religious leaders did believe in Jesusâ€“especially after he rose from the dead.
  * **Algunos de los lideres religiosos creian en Jesus-especialmente despues que resucito de la muerte.**
  * Many of the religious leaders believed that they were more righteous than other people, and they were too proud to admit their sins. They lied about Jesus to the Roman rulers and demanded that he be killed.
  * **Muchos de los lideres religiosos creian que ellos estaban correctos que otra gente, y estaban orgullosos de admitir sus pecados.**
  * Jesus condemned the Jewish leaders for being hypocrites. They claimed to know God, but did not obey him.
  * **Jesus condeno a los lideres Judios por ser hipocritas. Ellos reclamaron que conocian a Dios, pero no lo obedecian.**
