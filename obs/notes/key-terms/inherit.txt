====== inherit heredar ======

==== (to inherit, inheritance) heredar, herencia ====

===== Definition: Definición =====

When someone inherits something, he or she receives something valuable from someone else because of a special relationship with that person.

**Quando alguien hereda algo, el o ella recive algo valioso de alguien mas debido a una relacion especial con esa persona.**

   * **Inheritance from Family**  - After the death of a relative, sometimes family members receive money or property (items or land) that that relative owned before they died.
  * **Herencia de Familia - Despues de la muerte de un pariente, a veces miembros de familia reciven dinero o propiedad (artículos o tierra)****que al pariente poseía antes que ellos murieron.**
  * **Inheritance from God**  - God has promised to give certain things to his people. To the descendants of Abraham, he promised land. To those who trust in Jesus, he promised an inheritance of eternal (or everlasting) life with him in heaven.
[See also: **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:promised-land|Promised Land]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:04-06|[04-06]]]** When Abram arrived in Canaan God said, "Look all around you. I will give to you and your descendants all the land that you can see as an **inheritance**."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:27-01|[27-01]]]** One day, an expert in the Jewish law came to Jesus to test him, saying, "Teacher, what must I do to **inherit**  eternal life?"
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:35-03|[35-03]]]**"There was a man who had two sons. The younger son told his father, 'Father, I want my **inheritance**  now!' So the father divided his property between the two sons."
