====== Solomon  Salomón ======

===== Facts: =====

**Datos**

Solomon was the son of King David; his mother was Bathsheba.

**Salomón era el hijo del Rey David; su madre era Betzabé.**

   * When Solomon became king, God told him to ask for anything he wanted. So Solomon asked for wisdom to rule the people justly and well. God was pleased with Solomon's request and gave him both wisdom and much wealth.
  * **Cuando Salomón se hizo rey, Dios le dijo que le pidiera cualquier cosa que el quisiera.  Salomón pidió sabiduría para gobernar a su pueblo justamente y bien.  Dios le concedio a Salomón su solicitud y de dio sabiduría pero tambien muchas riquezas.**
  * Solomon built the Temple for God in Jerusalem that his father, David, had planned to build.
  * **Salmón construyo el Templo a Dios en Jerusalén, que su padre David había planeado construir.**
  * Although Solomon ruled wisely in the beginning years of his reign, later on he foolishly married many foreign women and started worshiping their gods.
  * **A pesar de que Salomón goberno sabiamente comenzando sus años de reinado, más tarde contrajo matrimonio neciamente con muchas mujeres extranjeras y comenzó a rendir culto a otros dioses.**
  * Because of Solomon's unfaithfulness, after his death, God divided the Israelites into two kingdoms that often fought against each other.
  * **Por la infidelidad de Salomón, luego de su muerte, Dios dividió a los israelitas en dos reinos que muchas veces pelearon entre sí.**
