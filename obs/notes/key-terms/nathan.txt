====== Nathan ======

Natan

===== Facts: =====

Datos:

Nathan was a faithful prophet of God who lived during the time when David was king over Israel.

Natan fue un fiel profeta de Dios que vivio durante el tiempo en que David era rey de Israel.

  * God sent Nathan to confront David after David sinned grievously against Uriah.
  * Dios envio a Natan para confrontar a David despues que David peco gravemente contra Urias.
  * Nathan rebuked David, in spite of the fact that David was the king.
  * Natan reprendio a David, sin importar que David era el rey.
  * David repented of his sin after Nathan spoke to him.
  * David se arrepintio de su pecado despues de que Natan hablara con el.

[See also: **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:david|David]]**, **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:uriah|Uriah]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-07|[17-07]]]** God sent the prophet **Nathan**  to David with this message, "Because you are a man of war, you will not build this Temple for me."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** God was very angry about what David had done, so he sent the prophet **Nathan**  to tell David how evil his sin was.
