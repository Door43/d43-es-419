====== descendant- descendiente ======

===== Definition: =====

**Definicion**:

A descendant is someone who is a direct blood relative of someone else further back in history.

**Un descendiente es alguien que es un familiar directo de alguien en el pasado**.

   * For example, Abraham was a descendant of Noah.
  * **Por ejemplo, Abran era descendiente de Noe.**
  * A person's descendants would be his children, grandchildren, great-great-grandchildren, etc.
  * **El descendiente de una persona serian sus hijos, nietos, bis-nietos,etc.**
