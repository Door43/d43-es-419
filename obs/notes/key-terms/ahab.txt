====== Ahab** **Ahab** **  ======

===== Facts: =====

Ahab was a very evil king who ruled over the northern kingdom of Israe

**Ahab era un rey malo que gobernaba la parte norte del reino de Israel.**

   * King Ahab was wicked and encouraged the people of the kingdom of Israel to worship false gods.
  * **El Rey Ahab era mavado y alentaba a la gente del reino de Israel a adorar a dioses falsos**.
  * The prophet Elijah confronted Ahab and told him that there would be a severe drought for three and a half years as a punishment for the sins that Ahab caused Israel to commit
  * **El profeta Elias confronto a Ahab y le dijo que iba  haber una sequia severa por tres y medio de anos como castigo por los pecados que Ahab habia causado a Israel a cometer.**
  * Ahab had 450 prophets to the false god named Baal. Elijah challenged those prophets and proved that Yahweh is the only true God.
  * **Ahab tenia 450 profetas [[para{{https://cdncache-a.akamaihd.net/items/it/img/arrow-10x10.png?nolink&0x10}}]] el falso dios llamado Baal. Elias reto a esos profetas y probo que Jehova  es el unico Dios.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-02|[19-02]]]** Elijah was a prophet when **Ahab** was king over the kingdom of Israel. **Ahab** was an evil man who encouraged people to worship a false god named Baal.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-03|[19-03]]]** **Ahab** and his army looked for Elijah, but they could not find him.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-05|[19-05]]]** After three and a half years, God told Elijah to return to the kingdom of Israel and speak with **Ahab** because he was going to send rain again.
