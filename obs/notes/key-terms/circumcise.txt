====== circumcise circuncidar ======

===== Definition: =====

Definicion:

To circumcise someone is to cut off the foreskin of that man or male child.

Circuncidar a alguien es cortar la piel de la punta del miembro viril a un hijo varon.

  * God commanded Abraham's family to circumcise every male among his family and servants as a sign of his covenant with them.
  * Dios ordeno a la familia de Abraham a que circuncidaran a todos los varones y sirvientes como un signo de su pacto con ellos.
  * God also commanded Abraham's descendants to continue to do this for every baby boy born into their households.
  * Dios tambien ordeno a los descendientes de Abraham a que continuaran haciendo esto con cada uno de los ninos que nacieran en sus casas.

  * **[[[05-05]]]** That day Abraham **circumcised**  all the males in his household.
