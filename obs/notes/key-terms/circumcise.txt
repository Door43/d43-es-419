====== circumcise __//circuncidar//__  ======

===== Definition: =====

Definición:

To circumcise someone is to cut off the foreskin of that man or male child.

**Circuncidar a alguien es cortar la piel de la punta del prepucio de tal hombre o su hijo varón.**

   * God commanded Abraham's family to circumcise every male among his family and servants as a sign of his covenant with them.
  * **Dios ordenó a la familia de Abraham a que circuncidaran a todos los varones y sirvientes como un signo de su pacto con ellos.**
  * God also commanded Abraham's descendants to continue to do this for every baby boy born into their households.
  * **Dios tambien ordenó a los descendientes de Abraham a que continuaran haciendo esto con cada uno de los niños que nacieran en sus casas.**
===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:05-03|[05-03]]]**"You must **circumcise**  every male in your family."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:05-05|[05-05]]]** That day Abraham **circumcised**  all the males in his household.
