====== justice **justicia**  ======

==== (justice, justly) ====


===== Definition: =====

**Definicion**

Justice is right or reasonable behavior or treatment.

**Justicia es el derecho o compartamiento rasonable o trato.**

   * Laws, actions, and decisions that are just are morally right and fair.
  * **Leyes, acciones, y decisiones que son solo moralmente correcta o justa.**
  * To rule justly is to make decisions that are morally right and to treat fairly those people over whom one rules.
  * **Para declarar con justicia es hacer decisiones que son moralmente correcta y tratan justamente a esa gente sobre las reglas.**
[See also: **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:judge|judge]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-09|[17-09]]]** David ruled with **justice** and faithfulness for many years, and God blessed him.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:18-13|[18-13]]]** Some of these kings (of Judah) were good men who ruled **justly** and worshiped God.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-16|[19-16]]]** They (the prophets) all told the people to stop worshiping idols and to start showing **justice** and mercy to others.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:50-17|[50-17]]]** Jesus will rule his kingdom with peace and **justice**, and he will be with his people forever.
