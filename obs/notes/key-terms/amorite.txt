====== Amorite **Amorreo**  ======

===== Facts: =====

The Amorites were a group of people living in Canaan in the time of Joshua.

**Los Amorreos eran un grupo de gente viviendo en los tiempos de Joshua.**

   * The Amorites lived in cities on both sides of the Jordan River. Each city was ruled by a king.
  * **Los Amorreos vivian en ciudades en ambos lados del Rio de Jordan. Cada ciudad era gobernada por un rey.**
  * God had promised to give the Israelites the land of Canaan, including the land of the Amorites.
  * **Dios habia prometido darle a los Israleistas la tierra de Cannan, incluyendo la tierra de los Amorreos.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:15-07|[15-07]]]** Sometime later, the kings of another people group in Canaan, the **Amorites**, heard that the Gibeonites had made a peace treaty with the Israelites, so they combined their armies into one large army and attacked Gibeon. 
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:15-08|[15-08]]]** In the early morning they surprised the **Amorite** armies and attacked them. 
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:15-09|[15-09]]]** God fought for Israel that day. He caused the **Amorites** to be confused and he sent large hailstones that killed many of the **Amorites**.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:15-10|[15-10]]]** God also caused the sun to stay in one place in the sky so that Israel would have enough time to completely defeat the **Amorites**.
