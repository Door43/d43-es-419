====== New Covenant Nuevo Pacto ======

===== Definition: =====

God made a covenant with Israel through his prophet Moses, and later promised that he would make a New Covenant.

**Dios hizo un pacto con Israel a travez de su profeta Moises, y luego prometio que el haria un Nuevo Pacto.**

   * The New Covenant is better than the old one because it is based on the sacrifice of Jesus for the sins of everyone.
  * **El Nuevo Pacto es mejor que el viejo porque este esta basado en el sacrificio de Jesus for los pecados de todo el mundo.**
  * This New Covenant came into effect when God's Son died and came back to life.
  * **Este Nuevo Pacto se hizo efectivo cuando el Hijo de Dios murio y resucito.**
  * Believers keep the New Covenant by trusting God to forgive them and save them because of the sacrificial death and resurrection of Jesus.
  * **Los creyentes mantienen el Nuevo Pacto por confiar en Dios para el perdon y la salvacion a causa de la muerte sacrificial y resurreccion de Jesus.**
  * God writes the New Covenant on the hearts of believers. As a result of this, Christians want to obey God, and they begin to live holy lives that demonstrate that God is working in them.
  * **Dios**** escribe el Nuevo Pacto en los corazones de los creyentes. Como resultado de esto, los Cristianos quieren obedecer a Dios, y empezar a vivir una vida santa que demuestra que Dios esta trabajando en ellos.**
  * The New Covenant will be fulfilled when God establishes his reign on earth. Everything will once again be very good, as it was at creation. There will be no more sin, suffering, or injustice. Instead, all of God's people will dwell with him in peace, love, and harmony.
  * **El Nuevo Pacto se cumplira cuando Dios establezca su reino en la tierra. Todo sera otra vez bueno, como fue en la creacion. Alli no habra mas pecado, sufrimiento, o injusticia, sino, Todo el pueblo de Dios vivira con El en paz, amor y armonia.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:21-05|[21-05]]]** Through the prophet Jeremiah, God promised that he would make a **New Covenant**, but not like the covenant God made with Israel at Sinai. In the **New Covenant**, God would write his law on the people's hearts, the people would know God personally, they would be his people, and God would forgive their sins. The Messiah would start the **New Covenant**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:21-14|[21-14]]]** Through the Messiah's death and resurrection, God would accomplish his plan to save sinners and start the **New Covenant**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:38-05|[38-05]]]** Then Jesus took a cup and said, "Drink this. It is my blood of the **New Covenant**  that is poured out for the forgiveness of sins. Do this to remember me every time you drink it."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:48-11|[48-11]]]** But God has now made a **New Covenant**  that is available to everyone. Because of this **New Covenant**, anyone from any people group can become part of God's people by believing in Jesus.
