====== sheep   oveja ======

===== Definition: =====

**Definición**:

A sheep is a medium-sized animal with four legs that has wool all over its body.

**Una oveja es un animal de tamaño mediano de cuatro patas que tiene lana cubriéndole todo su cuerpo.**

   * People eat meat from sheep and use their wool to make clothing and other things.
  * **Las personas comen carne de oveja y utilizan su lana para hacer ropa y otras cosas.**
  * Sheep are very trusting, weak and timid. They are easily influenced to wander away. They need a shepherd to lead them in the right direction and to provide them with food, water and shelter.
  * **Las ovejas son muy confiadas, débiles y tímidas.  Son fácilmente influenciadas a deambular o vagar.  Necesitan de un pastor que las conduzca en la dirección correcta y les provea comida, agua y abrigo.**
  * In the Bible, people are compared to sheep who have God as their shepherd.
  * **En la ** Biblia, las personas son comparadas con las ovejas que tienen a Dios como su pastor.

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-02|[17-02]]]** David was a shepherd from the town of Bethlehem. At different times while he was watching his father's **sheep**, David had killed both a lion and a bear that had attacked the **sheep**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:30-03|[30-03]]]** To Jesus, these people were like **sheep**  without a shepherd.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:38-08|[38-08]]]** Jesus said, "All of you will all abandon me tonight. It is written, 'I will strike the shepherd and all the **sheep**  will be scattered.'"
