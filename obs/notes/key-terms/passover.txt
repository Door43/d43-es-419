====== Passover  Pascua ======

===== Facts: =====

**Datos:**

This was a celebration that God wanted the Israelites to do each year in order to remember how God "passed over" the houses of the Israelites and did not kill their sons when he killed the firstborn sons of every Egyptian family.

**Esta era una celebración que Dios queria que los Israelitas celebraran cada año para recordar como Dios "pasó"  por las casas de los Israelitas y no mató a sus hijos cuando el mató a los primogenitos de cada familia egipcia.**

   * The Passover Feast, or celebration, includes a special meal that reminds people of the meal they ate that night. The meal included a lamb without any defect that they killed, roasted, and shared, as well as bread made without yeast.
  * **El Festival de la Pascua o la celebracion, incluia una cena especial que le recordaba a las personas la comida que comieron esa noche.  La comida incluía un cordero sin defecto que ellos mataban, asaban y lo compartían, así como el pan hecho sin levadura.**
  * God told the Israelites to eat this meal every year in order to remember and celebrate how God "passed over" their houses and how he set them free from slavery in Egypt.
  * **Dios le dijo a los israelitas que comieran esto todos los años para recordar y celebrar cuando Dios ¨pasó sobre¨ sus casas y como el los libertó de la esclavitud de Egipto.**
===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:12-14|[12-14]]]** God commanded the Israelites to remember his victory over the Egyptians and their deliverance from slavery by celebrating the **Passover**  every year.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:38-01|[38-01]]]** Every year, the Jews celebrated the **Passover**. This was a celebration of how God had saved their ancestors from slavery in Egypt many centuries earlier.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:38-04|[38-04]]]** Jesus celebrated the **Passover**  with his disciples.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:48-09|[48-09]]]** When God saw the blood, he passed over their houses and did not kill their firstborn sons. This event is called the **Passover**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:48-10|[48-10]]]** Jesus is our **Passover**  Lamb. He was perfect and sinless and was killed at the time of the **Passover**  celebration.
