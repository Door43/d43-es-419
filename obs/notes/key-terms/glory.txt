====== glory gloria ======

===== Definition: Definición: =====

Something or someone has glory when it is extremely good in some way.

**Algo o alguien tiene gloria cuando es extrémamente bueno en alguna area.**

   * Glory can refer to extreme greatness, power, or wealth.
  * **Gloria se refiere a excelencia, poder o riqueza extrema.**
  * Glory can also refer to the honor or reputation that someone has because of his extreme greatness.
  * **Gloria también se puede referir a el honor o reputación que alguien tiene por su excelencia.**
  * God possesses all glory. It is the outshining of his beauty, holiness, honor and power.
  * **Dios posee toda gloria. Es la amalgación de su belleza, santidad, honor y poder.**
  * To give God glory is to honor, praise, and worship him.
  * **Darle gloria a Dios es honrar, adorar y alabarlo.**
  * When we say that something brings God glory, it means that it leads people to honor, praise, and worship him.
  * **Cuando decimos que algo le trae gloria a Dios, significa que esa cosa dirige la gente a honrar, adorar y alabar a Dios.**
