====== prophet proféta ======

==== (prophet, prophecy, prophesy) ====

==== (proféta, profecía, profetizar) ====

===== Definition: =====

===== Definición: =====

A prophet is a person who speaks God's messages to people. He tells them what God wants them to know. God is the one who chooses and appoints the prophet to do this.

**Un proféta es una persona que trae un mensaje de Dios para la gente. El les dice lo que Dios quiere que ellos sepan. Dios es el que escoje y nombra a un proféta a que haga esto.**

   * "Prophet" could be translated as "God's spokesman."
  * **"Proféta" puede ser traducido como "Mensajero de Dios"**
  * A "prophecy" is the message that God tells the prophet to speak.
  * **Una "profecía" es un mensaje que Dios quiere que el profeta hable.**
  * To "prophesy" is the act of communicating God's message.
  * **"Profetizar" es el acto de comunicar el mensaje de Dios.**
  * Prophets told people about things that God had said would happen in the future.
  * **Los profétas hablaban de cosas que Dios había dicho que iban a pasar en el futuro.**
  * Prophets also warned people to turn away from their sins and obey God.
  * **Profétas también advertían a la gente que dejaran el pecado y que siguieran y obedecieran a Dios.**
  * There were also false prophets who claimed to speak messages from false gods such as Baal.
  * **También habían falsos profétas que pretendían hablar mensajes de Dios, pero eran mensajes de falsos dioses como del dios llamado Baal.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:12-12|[12-12]]]** When the Israelites saw that the Egyptians were dead, they trusted in God and believed that Moses was a **prophet**  of God.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** God was very angry about what David had done, so he sent the **prophet**  Nathan to tell David how evil his sin was.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-01|[19-01]]]** Throughout the history of the Israelites, God sent them **prophets**. The **prophets**  heard messages from God and then told the people God's messages.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-06|[19-06]]]** All the people of the entire kingdom of Israel, including the 450 **prophets**  of Baal, came to Mount Carmel.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-17|[19-17]]]** Most of the time, the people did not obey God. They often mistreated the **prophets**  and sometimes even killed them.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:21-09|[21-09]]]** The **prophet**  Isaiah **prophesied**  that the Messiah would be born from a virgin.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:43-05|[43-05]]]**"This fulfills the **prophecy**  made by the **prophet**  Joel in which God said, 'In the last days, I will pour out my Spirit.'"
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:43-07|[43-07]]]**"This fulfills the **prophecy**  which says, 'You will not let your Holy One rot in the grave.'"
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:48-12|[48-12]]]** Moses was a great **prophet**  who proclaimed the word of God. But Jesus is the greatest **prophet**  of all. He is the Word of God.
