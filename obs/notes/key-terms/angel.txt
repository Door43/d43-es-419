====== angel ángel ======

===== Definition: Definición =====

An angel is a powerful spirit being whom God created. These spirit beings exist to serve God by doing whatever he tells them to do. They serve God in different ways:

**Un ángel es un ser espiritual poderoso creado por Dios. Estos seres espirituales existen para servir a Dios haciendo todo lo que Él les dice. Ellos sirven a Dios en diferentes formas:**

   * They give people messages from God, including commands and other instructions.
  * **Ellos llevan mensajes de Dios a las personas, que incluyen órdenes y otras instrucciones.**
  * They protect and strengthen people.
  * **Ellos Protegen y fortalecen a las personas.**
  * They guard people and places.
  * **Ellos Vigilan personas y lugares.**
  *

Sometimes in the Bible "the angel of Yahweh" is used to refer to God himself.

**Algunas veces en la Biblia ¨el ángel de Jehová" se usa para referirse a Dios mismo.**

The opposite of "angel" is a "demon."

**Lo opuesto a "´angel" es un "demonio"**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:02-12|[02-12]]]** God placed large, powerful **angels**  at the entrance to the garden to keep anyone from eating the fruit of the tree of life.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:22-03|[22-03]]]** The **angel**  responded to Zechariah,"I was sent by God to bring you this good news."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:23-06|[23-06]]]** Suddenly, a shining **angel**  appeared to them (the shepherds), and they were terrified. The **angel**  said, "Do not be afraid, because I have some good news for you."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:23-07|[23-07]]]** Suddenly, the skies were filled with **angels**  praising God…
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:25-08|[25-08]]]** Then **angels**  came and took care of Jesus.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:38-12|[38-12]]]** Jesus was very troubled and his sweat was like drops of blood. God sent an **angel**  to strengthen him.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:38-15|[38-15]]]**"I could ask the Father for an army of **angels**  to defend me."
