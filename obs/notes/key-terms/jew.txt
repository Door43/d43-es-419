====== Jew Judio ======

==== (Jew, Jewish) ====


===== Facts: =====

**Datos:**

Jews are people who are descendants of Abraham through his grandson, Jacob.

**Los Judios eran gente descendientes de Abrahan a traves de su  nieto, Jacob.**

   * People began to call the Israelites "Jews" after they [[returned{{https://cdncache-a.akamaihd.net/items/it/img/arrow-10x10.png?nolink&0x10}}]] to Judah from their exile in Babylon.
  * **La gente empezaron a llamar a los Israelistas "Judios" despues que regresaron de Judah del exilio de Babilonia.**
  * The word "Jew" comes from the word "Judah." The Israelites who had been taken to Babylon were from the southern kingdom of Judah.
  * **La palabra"Judio" viene de la palabra "Judah". Los Israelistas que fueron cojidos de Babilonia eran del reino de Judah de la parte sur.**

  * Jesus the Messiah was Jewish. However, the Jewish religious leaders rejected Jesus and demanded that he be killed.
  * **Jesus el Mesias era Judio. Sin embargo, los lideres de la religion Judia****rechazaron a Jesus y demandaron que lo mataran.**
