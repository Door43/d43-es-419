====== resurrection  resurreción ======

===== Definition: =====

**Definición**:

Resurrection is the act of becoming alive again after having died.

**La resurreción es el acto de volver a la vida luego de haber muerto.**

   * To resurrect someone is to bring that person back to life again. Only God has the power to do this.
  * **Resucitar a alguien es traer una persona de vuelta a la vida.  Solo Dios tiene el poder de hacer esto.**
  * The word "resurrection" often refers to Jesus' coming back to life after he died.
  * **La palabra "resurreción¨ a menudo se refiere al regreso de Jesús a la vida despúes de haber muerto.**
  * When Jesus said, "I am the Resurrection and the Life" he meant that he is the source of resurrection, and the one who causes people to come back to life.
      * **Cuando Jesús dijo, ¨Yo soy la Resurreción y la Vida" quiso decir que El es la fuente de la resurreción, y el que puede causar que las personas vuelvan a la vida.**
  * Resurrection can be translated as, "coming back to life" or, "becoming alive again after being dead."
  * **Resurreción**  se puede traducir como, ¨volver a la vida¨o "volver a vivir despúes de haber estado muerto."

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:21-14|[21-14]]]** Through the Messiah's death and **resurrection**, God would accomplish his plan to save sinners and start the New Covenant.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:37-05|[37-05]]]** Jesus replied, "I am the **Resurrection**  and the Life. Whoever believes in me will live, even though he dies.
