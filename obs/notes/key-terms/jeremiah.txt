====== Jeremiah **Jeremias**  ======

===== Facts: =====

Jeremiah was a prophet of God in the kingdom of Judah.

**Jeremias era un profeta de Dios en el reino de Juda.**

   * Like most of the prophets, Jeremiah often had to warn the people that God was going to punish them for their sins.
  * **Como la mayoria de los profetas, Jeremias a menudo tenia que recordarle a la gente que Dios los iba a castigar por sus pecados.**
  * Jeremiah prophesied that the Babylonians would capture Jerusalem, which made some of the people of Judah angry. So they put him in a dry well and left him there to die. But the king of Judah ordered his servants to rescue Jeremiah from the well.
  * **Jeremias profesaba que los Babilonias iban a capturar a Jerusalem, que hizo algunas personas en Judah con coraje. Ellos cogieron y lo pusieron en pozo seco y lo dejaron para que se muriera. Pero el rey de Judah ordeno a sus sirvientes que  rescataran a Jeremias del pozo.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====


  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-17|[19-17]]]** Once, the prophet **Jeremiah** was put into a dry well and left there to die. He sank down into the mud that was in the bottom of the well, but then the king had mercy on him and ordered his servants to pull **Jeremiah** out of the well before he died.
  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:21-05|[21-05]]]** Through the prophet **Jeremiah**, God promised that he would make a New Covenant, but not like the covenant God made with Israel at Sinai.
