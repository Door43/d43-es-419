====== witness testigo ======

===== Definition: Definición =====

A witness is someone who has seen or heard something happen.

\\
**Un testigo es alguien que ha visto o escuchado algo que ha sucedido.**

   * During a trial, witnesses tell what they have seen or heard. What the witnesses say helps the judge to decide if the person being accused is guilty or innocent.
  * **Durante un proceso, un testigo dice lo que ha visto u oído.  Lo que el testigo dice ayuda al juez a decidir si la persona acusada es culpable o inocente.**
  * Witnesses are expected to tell the truth about what they have seen or heard.
  * **Se espera que los testigos digan la verdad sobre lo que han visto u oído. **
  * A witness who does not tell the truth about what he has seen or heard is called a, "false witness."
  * **Un testigo que no dice la verdad de lo que ha visto u oído  se le llama, ¨testigo falso".**
