====== God's law ======

La ley de DIos

===== Definition: =====

Definicion:

God's law is all of the commands and instructions that he gave to his people Israel.

La ley de Dios esta compuesta por todos los mandamientos e instrucciones que le dio a su pueblo Israel.

  * God gave his people many different commands and instructions when he spoke with Moses on Mount Sinai.
  * Dios dio a su pueblo muchos y diferentes mandamientos e instrucciones cuando hablo con Moises en el Monte Sinai.
  * The Ten Commandments were part of God's law. They were a concise statement of some of the most important responsibilities of Israel towards God and each other.
  * Los Diez Mandamientos son parte de la ley de Dios. Son instrucciones concisas y solidas de algunas de las responsabilidades mas importantes de Israel para con Dios y entre si mismos.
  * These commands deal with everyday things like worship, food, health, family relationships, and war.
  * Estos mandamientos se refieren a rutinas diarias como alabanza, alimentos, salud, relaciones familiares y guerra.
  * The laws that God gave to Israel were an expression of the moral nature of God. They teach us about the holy character of God, as well as the consequences of disobeying him.
  * Las leyes que Dios dio a Israel son una expresion de la naturaleza moral de Dios. Ellos nos ensenan del caracter santo de Dios, asi como tambien de las consequencias al desobedecerle.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:13-07|[13-07]]]** God also gave many other **laws**  and rules to follow. If the people obeyed these **laws**, God promised that he would bless and protect them. If they disobeyed them, God would punish them.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:13-09|[13-09]]]** Anyone who disobeyed **God's law**  could bring an animal to the altar in front of the Tent of Meeting as a sacrifice to God.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:15-13|[15-13]]]** Then Joshua reminded the people of their obligation to obey the covenant that God had made with the Israelites at Sinai. The people promised to remain faithful to God and follow **his laws**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:16-01|[16-01]]]** After Joshua died, the Israelites disobeyed God and did not drive out the rest of the Canaanites or obey **God's laws**.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:21-05|[21-05]]]** In the New Covenant, God would write **his law**  on the people's hearts, the people would know God personally, they would be his people, and God would forgive their sins.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:27-01|[27-01]]]** Jesus answered, "What is written in **God's law**?"
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:28-01|[28-01]]]** Jesus said to him, "Why do you call me 'good?' There is only one who is good, and that is God. But if you want to have eternal life, obey **God's laws**."
