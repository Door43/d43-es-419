====== idol idolo ======

===== Definition: =====

An idol is an object that people make so they can worship it.

**Un idolo es un objeto que la gente hace para poder alabarlo.**

   * People make idols to represent the gods that they worship.
  * **La gente hace los idolos para representar los dioses que ellos alaban.**
  * These false gods do not existâ€“there is no God besides Yahweh. Therefore the idols have no power
  * **Estos dioses falsos no existen-no hay otro Dios ademas de Jehova**.
  * Sometimes things happen by coincidence that make idols seem to have power. Sometimes demons work through idols in order to deceive people.
  * **Algunas veces las cosas pasan por coincidencia que los idolos hechos parecen tener poder. Algunas veces los demonios trabajan a traves de estos idolos en orden **para enganar a la gente.** **
  * Idols are often made of precious materials like gold or silver.
  * **Los idolos****a menudo son hechos con materiales de oro y plata.**

  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:13-05|[13-05]]]** "Do not make **idols** or worship them, because I, Yahweh, am a jealous God."

  ***[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-16|[19-16]]]** They (the prophets) all told the people to stop worshiping **idols** and to start showing justice and mercy to others.
