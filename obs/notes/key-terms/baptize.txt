====== baptize  bautizo ======

==== (to baptize, baptism) ====

el bautizo, bautizar

===== Definition: =====

To baptize someone is to ritually wash that person with water. This act is called, "baptism."

**El bautizo es una ceremonia en donde lavan a una persona con agua, esta costumbre se llama bautizo**

   * Baptism is a way for a person to demonstrate that they repent of their former life, and associate themselves with a certain religious group, teacher, or way of life.
  * **el bautizo es una manera que la persona puede demostrar arrepentimiento  de los hechos pasados, relacionado con ciertos groupos religiosos, o costumbres **
  * John (the Baptist) baptized people as a sign of repentance from their sins.
  * **Juan Bautista, bautizo personas como una senal de arrepentimiento de sus pecados**
  * Christian baptism is a representation of our hearts being cleansed from sin by faith in Jesus Christ, and of our entrance into the Church.
  * **bautizo christiano es una reprensentacion de nuestro corazon, purificandonos del pecado por el acto de fe en Jesucristo y nuestra entrada a la iglesia**
  * Christians vary in the way they do baptism. Some pour the water on the person, and others put the person completely under the water.
  * **Los Cristianos han cambiado la manera de bautizar, Unos ponen agua sobre la persona y otros te sumergen en el agua**.
[See also: **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:john-baptist|John (the Baptist)]]**, **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:repent|repentance]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:24-03|[24-03]]]** When people heard John's message, many of them repented from their sins, and John **baptized**  them. Many religious leaders also came to be **baptized**  by John, but they did not repent or confess their sins.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:24-06|[24-06]]]** The next day, Jesus came to be **baptized**  by John.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:24-07|[24-07]]]** John said to Jesus, "I am not worthy to **baptize**  you. You should **baptize**  me instead."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:42-10|[42-10]]]** So go, make disciples of all people groups by **baptizing**  them in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit and by teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:43-11|[43-11]]]** Peter answered them, "Every one of you should repent and be **baptized**  in the name of Jesus Christ so that God will forgive your sins."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:43-12|[43-12]]]** About 3,000 people believed what Peter said and became disciples of Jesus. They were **baptized**  and became part of the church at Jerusalem.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:45-11|[45-11]]]** As Philip and the Ethiopian traveled, they came to some water. The Ethiopian said, "Look! There is some water! May I be **baptized**?"
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:46-05|[46-05]]]** Saul immediately was able to see again, and Ananias **baptized**  him.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:49-14|[49-14]]]** Jesus invites you to believe in him and be **baptized**.
