====== demon-possessed **poseido-por el demonio**  ======

===== Definition: =====

A person who is demon-possessed has a demon or evil spirit that controls what he does and thinks.

**Una persona que es poseida por el demonio tiene un demonio o un espiritu malo que controla lo que el hace o piensa.**

   * Often a demon-possessed person will hurt himself or other people because the demon forces him to do that.
  * **A menudo una persona poseida por el demonio puede herise el mismo o otras personas porque el demonio lo fuerza  hacerlo.**
  * Jesus healed demon-possessed people by forcing the demons to come out of them.
  * **Jesus curo a gente poseida por un demonio obligando a los demonios a salirse de ellos.**
  * This could also be translated as, "demon-controlled" or, "controlled by a demon" or, "having a demon."
  * **Esto puede ser traducido como, "demonio-controlado o, controlado por un demonio."**
