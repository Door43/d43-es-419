==== (trust, to trust) ====

==== (confianza, confiar) ====

===== Definition: =====

===== Definición: =====

To trust someone or something is to believe that they are reliable, honest, true, and dependable.

**Confiar en alguien o en una cosa es creer que esa persona o cosa es responsable, honesta, leal y confiable.**

   * Trust is closely related to faith. We trust someone because we have faith that they will do what they promised to do.
  * **La confianza es relacionada estrechamente a la fé.**
  * Trusting in Jesus means believing that he is God, believing that his sacrificial death on the cross paid for our sins and depending on him to save us.
  * **Confiando en Jesús significa creyendo que él es Dios, creyendo que su muerte de sacrificio en la cruz pagó por nuestros pecados y dependiendo en él para salvarnos.**
  * Often people foolishly trust in themselves, other people, or things to meet their needs, even though these cannot bring them the good that they desire.
  * **A menudo, la gente tóntamente confían en ellos mismos, en otra gente, o en otras cosas para satisfacer sus necesidades, aunque estas cosas no pueden traerles el bién que ellos deséan.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:12-12|[12-12]]]** When the Israelites saw that the Egyptians were dead, they **trusted**  in God and believed that Moses was a prophet of God.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:14-15|[14-15]]]** Joshua was a good leader because he **trusted**  and obeyed God.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:17-02|[17-02]]]** David was a humble and righteous man who **trusted**  and obeyed God.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:34-06|[34-06]]]** Then Jesus told a story about people who **trusted**  in their own good deeds and despised other people.
