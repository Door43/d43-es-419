====== save  salvar ======

==== (save, salvation, saved) ====

(salvar, salvación, salvado)

===== Definition: =====

**Definición:**

To save someone or something is to rescue that person or thing from something bad.

**Salvar a alguien o algo es rescatar a esa persona o cosa de algo malo.**

   * In a physical sense, people can be saved from harm, danger, or death.
  * **En el sentido físico, las personas pueden salvarse de daños, peligro o muerte.**
  * In a spiritual sense, if a person has been "saved," it means that through Jesus' death on the cross, God has forgiven him and rescued him from being punished for his sin.
  * **En el sentido espiritual, si una persona es ¨salva¨, quiere decir que a través de la muerte de Jesucristo en la cruz, Dios le perdono y le rescato de ser castigado por su pecado.**
  * Only God can save people from being punished for their sin.
  * **Sólo Dios puede salvar a las personas de ser castigados por su pecado.**

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:09-08|[09-08]]]** Moses tried to **save**  his fellow Israelite.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:11-02|[11-02]]]** God provided a way to **save**  the firstborn son of anyone who believed in him.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:12-05|[12-05]]]** Moses told the Israelites, "Stop being afraid! God will fight for you today and **save**  you."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:12-13|[12-13]]]** The Israelites sang many songs to celebrate their new freedom and to praise God because he **saved**  them from the Egyptian army.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:16-17|[16-17]]]** This pattern repeated many times: the Israelites would sin, God would punish them, they would repent, and God would send a deliverer to **save**  them.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:44-08|[44-08]]]**"You crucified Jesus, but God raised him to life again! You rejected him, but there is no other way to be **saved**  except through the power of Jesus!"
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:47-11|[47-11]]]**] The jailer trembled as he came to Paul and Silas and asked, "What must I do to be **saved**?" Paul answered, "Believe in Jesus, the Master, and you and your family will be **saved**."
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:49-12|[49-12]]]** Good works cannot **save**  you.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:49-13|[49-13]]]** God will **save**  everyone who believes in Jesus and receives him as their Master. But he will not **save**  anyone who does not believe in him.
