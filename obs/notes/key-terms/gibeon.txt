====== Gibeon ======

Gibeon

==== (Gibeon, Gibeonite) ====

===== Facts: =====

Datos:

Gibeon was a city and a region in the land of Canaan where the Gibeonites lived.

Gibeon era una ciudad y una region en la tierra de Canaan donde vivian los Gibeonitas.

  * "Gibeonite" means "someone from the region called Gibeon."
  * La palabra "Gibeonita" significa: "alguien procedente de la region de Gibeon."
  * The Gibeonites had heard about how the Israelites had destroyed Jericho, and they were afraid. So the Gibeonites came to the leaders of Israel and pretended to be people from a far-away country.
  * Los Gibeonitas habian escuchado acerca de como los Israelitas habian desstruido Jerico, y tenian miedo.. Asi que los Gibeonitas se allegaron a los lideres de Israel pretendiendo ser de un pais muy lejano.
  * Israel made a treaty with the Gibeonites and did not destroy them.
  * Israel hizo un pacto con los Gibeonitas y no los destruyeron.

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:15-08|[15-08]]]** So Joshua gathered the Israelite army and they marched all night to reach the **Gibeonites**.
