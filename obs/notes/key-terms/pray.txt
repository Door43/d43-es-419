====== pray- orar ======

==== (pray, prayer) ====

**(orar, oración)**

===== Definition: =====

**Definición:**

To pray is to talk to God.

**Orar es hablar con Dios.**

   * People often pray to God to ask him to help them or other people.
  * **La gente a menudo ora a Dios para pedir ayuda para ellos o para otros.**
  * People also thank and praise God in their prayers.
  * **La gente además agradece y adora a Dios en sus oraciones.**
  * Praying can also mean confessing our sins to God and asking him to forgive us.
  * **Oración además significa confesar nuestros pecados a Dios y pedir que nos perdone**.
  * When people try to talk to their false god, this is also called "praying."
  * **También se le llama "oración" cuando la gente trata de hablarle a sus falsos dioses.**
===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:06-05|[06-05]]]** Isaac **prayed**  for Rebekah, and God allowed her to get pregnant with twins.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:13-12|[13-12]]]** But Moses **prayed**  for them, and God listened to his **prayer**  and did not destroy them.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:19-08|[19-08]]]** Then the prophets of Baal **prayed**  to Baal, "Hear us, O Baal!"
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:21-07|[21-07]]]** Priests also **prayed**  to God for the people.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:38-11|[38-11]]]** Jesus told his disciples to **pray**  that they would not enter into temptation.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:43-13|[43-13]]]** The disciples continually listened to the teaching of the apostles, spent time together, ate together, and **prayed**  with each other.
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:49-18|[49-18]]]** God tells you to **pray**, to study his word, to worship him with other Christians, and to tell others what he has done for you.
