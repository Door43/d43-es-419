====== John (the Baptist) **Juan (el Bautista)**  ======

===== Facts: =====

**Datos:**

John was the son of Zechariah and Elizabeth. Since "John" was a common name, he is often called "John the Baptist" to distinguish him from the other people named John, such as the Apostle John

**Juan era hijo de Zacarias y Elizabeth**. Como "Juan" era un nombre comun, muchas veces lo llamaban "Juan el Bautista" para distinguirlo a el de otra gente llamadas Juan, tal como Juan el Apostol.

   * John was the prophet whom God sent to prepare people to believe in and follow the Messiah.
  * **Juan era un profeta quien Dios mando para preparar a la gente a creer y seguir al Mesias.**
  * John told people to confess their sins, turn to God, and stop sinning, so that they would be ready to receive the Messiah.
  * **Juan le dijo a la gente que confesaran sus pecados y buscaran a Dios, y parar de pecar, asi podian estar preparados para recivir al Mesias.**
  * John baptized many people in water as a sign that they were sorry for their sins and were turning away from them.
  * **Juan bautizo mucha gente en agua como una senal que estaban arrenpentidos por sus pecados y estaban alejandose de ellos.**
  * John was called, "John the Baptist" because he baptized many people.
  * **Juan lo llamaban, "Juan el Bautista" ****p**orque el bautizo a mucha gente.
  * Make sure that people will understand that "the Baptist" does not refer to the church by this name. It may be necessary to say instead, "John the Baptizer" or, "John who baptizes."
  * **Asegurate que la gente va a entender que "el Bautista" no se refiere a la iglesia por su nombre. Puede ser necesario decir " Juan el Bautista" o, "Juan el que bautiza"**
