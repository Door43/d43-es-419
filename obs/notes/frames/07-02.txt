===== Esau Sold His Rights [07-02] *B =====

<font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** Esaú vende sus derechos [07-02]  **</font>

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-02.jpg?nolink&}}

One day, when **Esau** came back from hunting, he was very hungry. Esau said to **Jacob**, “Please **give me some of the food** you have made.” Jacob responded, “First, **give me your rights as the oldest son**.” So Esau gave Jacob his **rights as the oldest son**. Then Jacob gave him some food.

Un día, cuando **Esaú**volvió de cazar, tenía mucha hambre. **Esaú** dijo a **Jacob**: “Por favor, dame un poco de la comida que has hecho.” **Jacob** respondió: “En primer lugar, dame tus derechos como el hijo mayor.” Así,** Esaú** dio **Jacob** a sus derechos del hijo mayor. Entonces, **Jaco**b le dio un poco de comida.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **give me some of the food … give me your rights**  – Some languages may prefer to use two different words for "give" here. This would also apply to, "Esau gave … Jacob gave" in the last two sentences.
  * **dame algo de la comida … dame tus derechos - **  Algunos lenguajes pueden preferir usar dos diferentes palabras para "dar" aqui. Esto también se aplicaria a, "Esau dio … Jacob dio" en las dos ultimas frases.
  * **rights as the oldest son**  - According to their custom, since Esau was the oldest son, he should have been the one to receive twice as much of their father's wealth when their father died. Jacob thought of a way to take these rights of the oldest son from Esau.
  * **derechos como el hijo mayor -**  De acuerdo a sus costumbres, ya que Esau era el hijo mayor, el debería haber sido el que recibiera el doble de las riquezas cuando su padre muera. Jacob pensó una forma de tomar para el estos derechos del hijo mayor que pertenecían a Esau.
