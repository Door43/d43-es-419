===== Jesus Died [40-07] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **Jesus** cried out, “**It is finished**! **Father**, I give my **spirit into your hand**s.” Then he **bowed his head** and **gave up his spirit**. When he **died**, there was an earthquake and the **large curtain** that separated the people from the presence of **God** in the **Temple** was torn in two, from the top to the bottom.

Entonces, Jesús gritó, “¡Está terminado!” Padre, doy mi espíritu en tus manos.” Entonces, se inclinó su cabeza y entregó su espíritu. Cuando él murió, hubo un teremoto y la cortina grande en el templo​ que separaba la gente de la presencia de Dios fue partida en dos, de arriba hasta abajo.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It is finished!**  - This could also be translated as, "It is completed" or, "I have finished it" or, "I have completed the work." This means that Jesus' work of salvation was complete.
  * **Esta teminado! - Esto puede ser traducido como, "Esta completado" o, " Yo lo termine" o, " Yo he completado el trabajo." Esto significa el trabajo de Jesus fue completado.**
  * **into your hands**  - That is, "into your care."
  * **en tus manos - Esto es, "en su cuidado."**
  * **bowed his head**  - That is, "lowered his head."
  * **inclino su cabeza - Esto es,"bajo su cabeza."**
  * **gave up his spirit**  - That is, "gave up his spirit to God" or, "released his spirit to God and died."
  * **entrego su espiritu - ** Esto es,"dio su **espiritu a Dios."**
  * **large curtain**  - This was a large, strong fabric hung in the temple. It was like a wall separating one room from another. It can also be translated as "thick curtain" or, "hanging fabric" or, "screen.
  * **cortina grande - Esto era una fuerte grande tela  colgando en el templo. Era como una pared separando un cuarto de otro. Puede ser tambien traducido como una "cortina gruesa" o, "tela colgando" o, "cortina."**
