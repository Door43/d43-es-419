===== The Ministry of Elisha [26-06] El ministerio de Eliseo =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-06.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** continued saying, “And during the time of the **prophet Elisha**, there were many people in **Israel** with skin diseases. But Elisha did not **heal** any of them. He only healed the skin disease of **Naaman**, **a commander** of Israel’s enemies.” The people who were listening to Jesus were **Jews**. So when they heard him say this, **they were furious at him**.

Jesús continuó diciendo: “Y durante el tiempo del profeta Eliseo, había mucha gente en Israel con enfermedades de la piel. Pero Eliseo no sanó a ninguna de ellas. Él sólo sanó la enfermedad de la piel de Naamán, el comandante de los enemigos de Israel.” Las personas que escuchaban a Jesús eran Judios. Así que, cuando le oyeron decir esto, se enfurecieron contra él.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Elisha**  - Elisha was God's prophet who came after Elijah. Like Elijah, Elisha confronted Israelite kings who were sinning against God and he did miracles that God gave him power to do.
  * **Eliseo **- Eliseo era un profeta de Dios que vino despues de Elias, como Elias, Eliseo confronto a los reyes Israelitas quienes estaban pecando en contra de Dios y el hizo milagros que Dios le dio poder para hacer.
  * **a commander**  - That is, "a military leader."
  * **un comandante **- Esto es, "un lider militar."
  * **they were furious at him**  - The Jews did not want to hear that God had blessed any people group besides themselves, so they were very angry at what Jesus said.
  * **ellos estaban furiosos a el **- Los Judios no querian oir que Dios estaba bendiciendo a otro grupo de gente ademas de ellos, asi que ellos tenian mucho coraje con lo que dijo Jesus.
