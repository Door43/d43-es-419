==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **Israelites**  - Here, "Israelites" refers only to the people living in the northern kingdom of Israel, not to the people living in the southern kingdom of Judah.
  * **Israelitas -**  Aqui, "Israelitas" se refiere solamente a la gente viviendo en la parte norte del reino de Israel, no a la gente viviendo en la perte sur del reino de Juda.
