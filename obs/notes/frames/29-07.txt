===== The King Heard [29-07] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-07.jpg?nolink&}}

“Some other **servants** saw **what had happened** and **were greatly disturbed**. They went to the **king** and told him **everything**.”

“Algunos otros siervos vieron lo que había sucedido y se vieron muy perturbados. Fueron al rey y le dijeron todo.”

===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:servant|servants]]**
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:king|king]]**
===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * **what had happened**  - That is, "that the servant had refused to cancel the other servant's debt and had put him into prison."
  * lo que habia pasado - Esto es, "que el sirviente habia rehusado cancelar la deuda de el otro sirviente y lo puso en prision."
  * **were greatly disturbed**  - That is, "deeply grieved" or, "very distressed."
  * fueron grandemente disturbados - Esto es, "profundamente apenado" o, " muy consternados"
  * **everything**–That is, "they told the king what the servant had done to his fellow servant.
