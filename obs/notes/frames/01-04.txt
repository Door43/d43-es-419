===== God Created the Land [01-04]  *B =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-04.jpg?nolink&}}

On the **third day**, **God spoke** and separated the water from the dry land. He called the dry land “**earth**,” and he called the water “seas.” God saw that what he had **created** was **good**.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **third day**  - The next in the series of orderly days in which God prepared the earth for life.
  * **tercer día - El próximo en la serie de días ordenados en el cual Dios preparó la tierra para la vida.**
  * **God spoke**  - God created the dry land by speaking a command.
  * **Dios habló - Dios creó la tierra seca dando una orden.**
  * **earth**  - This word is used here to refer to dirt or soil, which is what dry land is made of. The word "earth" in **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:01-01|[01-01]]]** refers to the whole world that people live on.
  * **tierra - Esta palabra es usada aquí para referirse al barro, que es de lo que la tierra seca se creó. La palabra "tierra" en [01-01] se refiere a todo el mundo donde la gente vive.**
  * **created**  - This word is used here in the sense of making something out of nothing.
  * **creó - Esta palabra es usada aquí en el sentido de materializar algo de la nada.**
