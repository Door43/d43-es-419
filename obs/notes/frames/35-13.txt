===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * (Jesus continua la historia.)
  * **it is right for us**  - That is, "it is the right thing for us to do" or, "it is proper for us."
  * **Si es correcto para nosotros**  - "Es lo correcto para nosotros hacer" o, "es apropiado para nosotros."
  * **your brother**  - The father refers to his younger son as, "your brother" to remind the older son of his relationship to the younger son and of how he should love his younger brother.
  * **tu hermano **- El padre se refiere a su hijo menor como, "tu hermano" para recordarle a su hijo mayor de su relacion con su hermano menor y como el debe amar a su hermano menor.
