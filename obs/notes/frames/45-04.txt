===== Stephen Was Stoned [45-04] / Esteban Fue Apedreado =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-04.jpg?nolink&}}

When the **religious leaders** heard this, they were so angry that they **covered their ears** and **yelled loudly**. They dragged Stephen out of the city and threw stones at him in order to kill him.

Cuando los líderes religiosos oyeron esto, estaban tan enojados que cubrieron sus orejas y le gritaron de voz en cuello. Ellos arrastaron a Esteban afuera de la ciudad y le tiraron piedras para matarle.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **covered their ears**  - This could also be translated as, "They put their hands over their ears." This was to show that they did not want to hear what Stephen said.
  * **cubrieron sus oidos - ** Esto puede ser traducido como**, **"Ellos pusieron sus manos sobre sus orejas." Esto es para mostrar que ellos no querian escuchar lo que Estaban dijo**."**
  * **yelled loudly**  - They were yelling in anger. Translate this in a way that communicates that they were very upset
  * **grito en voz alta - ** Ellos estaban gritando con ira. Traduce esto en una forma que comuniques que ellos estaban bien molestos.
