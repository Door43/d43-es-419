===== An Angel Spoke to Mary [22-04] Un Ángel le Habló a María *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-04.jpg?nolink&}}

When **Elizabeth** was **six months pregnant**, the same **angel** suddenly appeared to **Elizabeth’s relative**, whose name was **Mary**. She was a **virgin** and **was engaged** to be married to a man named **Joseph**. The angel said, “You will become pregnant and give birth to a son. You are to name him **Jesus**. He will be the **Son** of the **Most High God** and will rule forever.”

Cuando** Elizabeth ** estaba** embarazada de seis meses**, el mismo **ángel** apareció de repente al pariente de** Isabel**, que se llamaba **María**. Ella era virgen y **//estaba comprometida//** para casarse con un hombre llamado **José**. El ángel dijo: “Vas a quedar embarazada y dar a luz a un hijo. Vas a llamarlo **Jesús**. Él será el **Hijo** del **Dios Altísimo** y gobernará para siempre.”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **six months pregnant**  - Either she had already been pregnant for six months or she was in the sixth month of her pregnancy.
  * **seis meses de embarazo**  - O ella ya había embarazada por seis meses o ella estaba en el sexto mes de su embarazo.
  * **pregnant**  - Different languages have different idioms to talk about pregnancy, such as "she was with child" or, "she had one in her body" or, "she had belly." Some languages have special ways of speaking about it politely, such as "she was expecting." Use an expression that will not be embarrassing to readers
  * **embarazo**  - Diferente idiomas tenían diferentes modismos de hablar sobre el embarazo, como "ella está embarazada, "ella tiene uno en su cuerpo" o, "tiene barriga".Algunos idiomas tienen una forma especial de decirlo educadamente, como "ella esta esperando. "Usan una expresión que no averguenzan a los lectores
  * **Elizabeth**  - She was Zechariah's wife. An angel told Zechariah that Elizabeth would give birth to a son.
  * **Isabel- Ella era la esposa de Zacarías. Un ángel le dijo a ZacarÍas que Isabel iba a darle nacimiento a un hijo.**
  * **Elizabeth's relative**  - Many translations say, "cousin" here but we don't know exactly how these two women were related. A general term like, "relative," "kin" or, "cousin" could be used.
  * **pariente de Isabel **- Muchas traducciones dicen, "prima" aqui no sabemos exactamente como estas dos mujeres estaban relacionadas. Un termino general como, "relativo" o, "parientes" o, "prima" pueden usarse.
  * **was engaged**  - That is, "was promised."
  * **estaba prometida**- Esto es, " estaba comprometida."
