===== An Angel Spoke to Mary [22-04] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-04.jpg?nolink&}}

When **Elizabeth** was **six months pregnant**, the same **angel** suddenly appeared to **Elizabeth’s relative**, whose name was **Mary**. She was a **virgin** and **was engaged** to be married to a man named **Joseph**. The angel said, “You will become pregnant and give birth to a son. You are to name him **Jesus**. He will be the **Son** of the **Most High God** and will rule forever.”

Cuando Elizabeth estaba embarazada de seis meses, el mismo ángel apareció de repente al pariente de Isabel, que se llamaba María. Ella era virgen y estaba comprometida para casarse con un hombre llamado José. El ángel dijo: “Vas a quedar embarazada y dar a luz a un hijo. Vas a nombrarle Jesús. Él será el Hijo del Dios Altísimo y reinará para siempre.”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **six months pregnant**  - Either she had already been pregnant for six months or she was in the sixth month of her pregnancy.
  * **seis meses de embarazo - Uno u otro ella ya estaba embarazada por seis meses o ella estaba en el sexto mes de su embarazo.**
  * **pregnant**  - Different languages have different idioms to talk about pregnancy, such as "she was with child" or, "she had one in her body" or, "she had belly." Some languages have special ways of speaking about it politely, such as "she was expecting." Use an expression that will not be embarrassing to readers
  * **embarazo - Diferente idiomas tenian diferentes modismos de hablar sobre el embarazo**, **como "ella esta embarazada,**** "ella tiene uno en su cuerpo" o, "tiene berriga".****Algunos idiomas tienen una forma especial de decirlo educadamente, como "ella esta esperando. "Usan una expresion que no averguenzan a los lectores**
  * **Elizabeth**  - She was Zechariah's wife. An angel told Zechariah that Elizabeth would give birth to a son.
  * **Elizabeth - Ella era la esposa de Zacarias. Un Angel le ** dijo **a Zacarias****que Elizabeth iba a darle nacimiento a un hijo.**
  * **Elizabeth's relative**  - Many translations say, "cousin" here but we don't know exactly how these two women were related. A general term like, "relative," "kin" or, "cousin" could be used.
  * **la familia de Elizabeth - Muchas traducciones dicen, "prima" aqui no sabemos exactamente como estas dos mujeres estaban relacionadas. Un termino general como, "relativo" o, "parientes" o, "prima" pueden usarse.**
  * **was engaged**  - That is, "was promised."
  * **estaba prometido- Esto es, " estaba compremetida."**
