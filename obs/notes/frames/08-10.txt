===== The Famine [08-10] / La hambruna =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-10.jpg?nolink&}}

**The famine was severe** not only in **Egypt**, but also in **Canaan** where **Jacob** and his family lived.

El hambre era muy severa no sólo en **Egipto**, pero también,en** Canaan ** dónde **Jacob** y su familia vivían.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **Egypt, but also Canaan**  – For some languages it may be clearer or more natural to say, "the country of Egypt, but also the land of Canaan."
  * **Egipcio, pero tambien Canaán - Para algunas lenguajes podria ser mas claro o mas natural decir, " el pais de Egipcio, pero tambien la tierra de Canaán."**
  * **The famine was severe**  - The famine was very bad. There was very little food and many people outside of Egypt were starving.
  * **la hambrura era severa - La hambrura era muy mala. Habia muy poca comida y mucha jente afuera de Egipcio estaban muriendo de hambre.**
