===== Make Us an Idol for Us/Haz Un Idolo Para Nosotros [13-11] *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-11.jpg?nolink&}}

For many days, **Moses** was on top of **Mount Sinai** talking with **God**. **The people became tired of waiting** for him to return. So they **brought gold** to **Aaron** and asked him to make an **idol** for them!

**Por muchos días, Moisés ** estaba en la cima del **Monte de** **Sinaí** hablando con **Dios**. La gente se cansó de esperar su regreso. ¡Así, **ellos llevaron oro** a **Aarón** y le pidieron que les hiciera un **ídolo** para ellos!

==== Translation Notes: Notas de traducción ====

  * **The people became tired of waiting**  - This could be translated as, "The people became impatient because he did not return quickly" or, "The people didn't want to wait any longer for him to return."
  * **La gente se cansó de esperar**  - Esto puede ser traducido como, "La gente se pusieron impaciente porque èl no regresó rápidamente" o, "La gente no quizo esperar más a que el regresara".
  * **brought gold**  - These were objects and jewelry made of gold, which could be melted and formed into other things.
  * **llevaron oro **- Estos eran objetos y joyas hechas de oro, que podían ser derretidas para formar otras cosas.
