===== Jesus Will Reign Forever [50-17]Jesús Reinará para Siempre [50-17] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-17.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** and his people will live on the new earth, and he will reign forever over everything that exists. He will **wipe away every tear** and **there will be no more suffering**, **sadness**, **crying**, **evil**, **pain**, **or death**. Jesus will **rule his kingdom with peace and justice**, and he will be with his people forever.

Jesús y su gente vivrán en la nueva tierra, y él reinará para siempre sobre toda cosa que existe. El enjugará toda lagrima y no habrá más sufrimiento, tristeza, llanto, ni muerte. Jesús reinará sobre su reino con paz y justicia, y él estará con su gente para siempre.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **wipe away every tear**  - This could also be translated as, "bring an end to all of our grief" or, "make trouble end" or, "tenderly take away any sadness people have."
  * **limpiar cada lagrima -**  Esto tambien puede ser traducido como, "traer un final a toda nuestra pena" o, "hacer problema terminar" o, "tiernamente quitar cualquier tristesa gente tiene."
  * **there will be no more suffering, sadness, crying, evil, pain, or death**  - This could also be translated as, "people will no longer suffer, be sad, cry, do evil things, feel pain, or die."
  * **no va haber mas sufrimiento, tristesa, llanto, mal, dolor, o muerte - **  Esto tambien puede ser traducido como, "gente no mas va a sufrir, estar triste, llorar, hacer mala cosas, sentir dolor, o morir."
  * **rule his kingdom with peace and justice**  - This could also be translated as, "rule over his people fairly, in a way that brings them peace."
  * **governar su reino con paz y justicia - **  Esto tambien puede ser traducido como, "governar sobre su gente justamente, in un modo que les traerá paz."
