===== Joshua Renewed the Covenant / Josué restablecio el pacto [15-13] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-13.jpg?nolink&}}

**When Joshua was an old man**, he called all the people of **Israel** together. Then Joshua reminded the people of their obligation to **obey** the **covenant** that **God** had made with the **Israelites** at **Sinai**. The **people promised** to **remain faithful to God** and **follow his laws**.

Cuando Josué era un hombre viejo, llamó a todo el pueblo de Israel juntos. Entonces, Josué les recordó a los habitantes de su obligación de obedecer el pacto que Dios había hecho con los israelitas en el Sinaí. Las personas se comprometieron a permanecer fieles a Dios y seguir sus leyes.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **When Joshua was an old man**  – It may be clearer to say, "Many years later, when Joshua was an old man." Joshua was over 100 years old at this time.
  * **Quando Josué era un hombre viejo - Puede que sea claro decir, " Muchos años despues, quando Josué era un hombre viejo." Josué tenia mas de cien años en este tiempo.**
  * **remain faithful to God**  - In other words, they would be loyal to God. They would worship and serve only God; they would not worship or serve any other gods.
  * **permanecer fiel a Dios - En otra palabras, ellos serian fieles a Dios. Ellos adorarian y servirian nomas a Dios; ellos no adorarian o servirian nigun otro Dioses. **
  * **follow his laws**  - This means that the people would obey the laws that God had given them already as part of the covenant.
  * **segir sus leyes - Esto significa que la gente obedecerian la leyes que Dios les habia dado ya como parte de la alianza.**
