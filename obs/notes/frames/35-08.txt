===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * (Jesus continua la historia.)
  * **sinned against God and against you**  - This could be translated as, "sinned against God, and I have also sinned against you."
  * **peque en contra de Dios y en contra tuya **- Esto puede ser traducido como, "peque en contra de Dios y tambien peque en contra tuya."
  * **I am not worthy**  - It is also possible to say, "So I am not worthy" or, "As a result, I am not worthy."
  * **Yo no soy digno **- Esto es tambien posible decir, " Asi yo no soy digno" o, "Como resultado no soy digno."
