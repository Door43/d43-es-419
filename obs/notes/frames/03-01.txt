**After a long time**, many people were living in the world. They had become **very wicked and violent**. **It became so bad** that **God decided to destroy** the whole world with a **huge flood**.

**Despues de un largo tiempo, mucha gente estaban viviendo en el mundo. Ellos se convirtieron muy malvados y violentos. Se convirtio tan malo que Dios ****deci****dio**** destruir** el **mundo** completo **con una enorme inundacion.**

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **After a long time**  - This story happens many generations (hundreds of years) after creation.
  * **Despues de un cierto tiempo- Esta historia sucedio muchas generaciones (cientos de anos) despues de la creacion.**
  * **very wicked and violent**  - It may be more natural to say, "had become wicked and did violent things".
  * **muy perverso y violento-es mejor decir natural, "se convirtio perverso y cometieron cosas violentas."**
  *
  * **It became so bad**  â€“ It may be clearer to say, "People were behaving in such harmful and evil ways that God."
  * **Se convirtio en lo malo-Es mejor decir claramente, "Que la gente se estaba comportando de una forma perjudicial y mala."**
  * **God decided to destroy**  - This does not mean that the earth would be destroyed completely. Rather God planned to destroy all the people who had rebelled against him and who had caused such evil and violence. This flood would also kill all the land animals and the birds.
  * **Dios decidio destruir-Esto no significa que la tierra iba a ser completamente destruida. Mejor dicho Dios planifico destruir toda la gente que se habia rebelado en contra de el y que habian causado el mal y violencia, El diluvio tampien iba a matar a todos los animales y los pajaros.**
  * **huge flood**  - Very deep water that would cover the earth, even in places where the ground was normally dry, and even covering the tops of the highest mountains.
  * **grande diluvio- Aguas bien profundas que podrian cubrir la tierra, incluso en sitios donde la tierra era normalmente seca. hasta cubriendola el sumo de las montanas.**
