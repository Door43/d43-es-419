===== The Message of the Prophets [19-18]/El Mensage de los Profetas [19-18]*F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-18.jpg?nolink&}}

The **prophets continued to speak for God** even though the people hated them. They warned people that God would destroy them if they did not **repent**. They also reminded people **of the promise that God’s Messiah would come**.

Los **profetas ****siguieron hablando por ******Dios** ** a pesar de que la gente los odiaba. Ellos advirtieron a la gente que Dios los destruiría a ellos si no se **arrepentían**. También, les recordó a la gente **de la** **promesa ** que el **Mesías ** de **Dios vendrá**.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **continued to speak for God**  - That is, "continued to tell the people what God wanted to tell them."
  * **siguieron hablando de Dios -**  Eso es, "continuaron diciendo a la gente lo que Dios queria decirle a ellos."
  * **of the promise that God's Messiah would come**  – This could be translated as, "that God had promised that his Messiah would come to save his people."
  * **una de las promesas era que el Mesías de Dios vendrá - **  Esto se puede traducir como, "que Dios habia prometido que el Mesias iba a venir a salvar su gente."
