===== The Seed on the Path [33-02]  / La Semilla sobre el Camino =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-02.jpg?nolink&}}** Jesus** told this story. “A farmer went out to plant **some seed**. As he was **spreading the seeds by hand**, some seeds happened to fall on the path, and the birds came and ate all of those seeds.”

**Jesús ** les contó esta historia. “Un agricultor salió para sembrar unas semillas. Mentras estaba esparciendo las semillas a mano, algunas semillas por causualidad se cayeron en el camino, y las aves llegaron y comieron todas aquellas semillas.”
===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**
===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **spreading the seeds by hand**  - That is, "throwing the seeds on the soil" or, "covering all of his crop soil with seeds." This is the way that farmers in the ancient Middle East typically planted grain-bearing crops.
  * **difusion de semillas con la mano**  - Esto es, "lanzando las semillas en el suelo" o, "cubriendo toda la cosecha con semillas." Esto es una forma que los agricultores en el antiguo Oriente planta el cultivo de grano."
