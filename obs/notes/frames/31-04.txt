===== The Disciples Were Afraid / Los Discípulos tenían miedo [31-04] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-04.jpg?nolink&}}

The **disciples** were very afraid when they saw **Jesus**, because they thought they were seeing **a ghost**. Jesus knew that they were afraid, so he called out to them and said, “**Do not be afraid. It is I**!”

Los discípulos estuvieron muy asustados cuando vieron a Jesús, porque pensaron que estuvieron viendo un fantasma. Jesús supo que estuvieron temerosos, así, les llamó y les dijo, “No tengan miedo. ¡Soy yo!”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a ghost**  - That is, "a spirit." They thought Jesus was a spirit, since normally a human being cannot walk on water.
  * **un fantasma **- Eso es, " un espíritu." ellos pensaron que Jesús era un espíritu, porque normalmente un humano no puede caminar en agua
  * **Do not be afraid**  - For some languages it may be more natural to say here, "Stop being afraid."
  * **No tengan miedo **- en algunas lenguas es mas natural decir, "Paren no tengan miedo"
  * **it is I!**  - For some, it might be more natural to translate this as, "It is me, Jesus."
  * **soy yo!**  - para algunos puede ser mas natural traducir esto como, "Soy yo Jesús"
