===== Israel Left Egypt [12-01] / Israel salio de Egipto [12-01] F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-01.jpg?nolink&}}

The **Israelites** were very happy to leave **Egypt**. **They were no longer slaves**, and they were **going** to the **Promised Land**! The **Egyptians** gave the Israelites whatever they asked for, even gold and silver and other valuable things. Some people from other nations **believed** in **God** and went along with the Israelites as they left Egypt.

Los **Israelitas** estaban muy contentos para salir de** Egipto**. ¡Ya no eran **esclavos**, y estaban yendose para la** tierra prometida**! Los **Egipcios ** les dieron a los **Israelitas** cualquier cosa que ellos pidieron, aun el oro, la plata y las otras cosas de valor. Algunas personas de otras naciones creyeron en** Dios ** y fueron con los** Israelitas ** mientras salieron de **Egipto**.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **They were no longer slaves**  - This could be translated as, "They were not slaves anymore."
  * **Ellos ya no eran esclavos**  - Esto se puede traducir como, "Ellos ya no eran esclavos."
  * **going**  – Some languages may use a more specific word like "traveling" since they would be going a long distance to the Promised Land.
  * **yendo**
 - Algunos idiomas pueden usar una palabra mas especifica como,"viajando" Ellos estarian iendo desde una larga distancia a la Tierra prometida.
  * **Promised Land**  - This is the land that God had promised that he would give to Abraham's descendants.
  * **Tierra Prometida**  - Esto es la tierra que Dios habia prometido que el le iba a dar a los descendientes de Abraham.
