===== The Wicked Servant [29-08] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-08.jpg?nolink&}}

“The **king called the servant** and said, ‘You wicked servant! I **forgave** your debt because you **begged me**. **You should have done the same**.’ The king was so angry that he **threw** the **wicked** servant into prison until he could pay back all of his debt.”

“El rey mandó llamar al siervo y le dijo:” ¡Siervo malvado! Le perdoné su deuda porque me lo suplicó. Usted debería haber hecho lo mismo. 'El rey estaba tan enojado que él arrojó al mal siervo en la cárcel hasta que pagara todo su deuda ”.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * **called the servant**  - That is, "commanded the servant to come to him" or, "ordered his guards to bring the servant to him."
  * **llamo al sirviente**  - esto es, " manso al sirviente a venir" o, ordeno a sus sirvientes que le trajeran el otro sirviente."
  * **begged me**  - This could be translated as, "pleaded with me" or, "urgently asked me to be merciful."
  * **me suplicaste**  - Esto podria ser traducido como, "me imploraste" o, "insistentemente me pediste que tuviera misericordia"
  * **You should have done the same**  - That is, "You should have forgiven the man who owed you, just as I forgave you."
  * **Usted deberia haver hecho lo mismo**  - Esto es, "Usted deberia haber perdonado al hombre que le debia, asi como yo le perdone a Usted."
  * **threw**  - That is, "commanded his guards to put." Consider how "threw" was translated in frame **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:29-06|[29-06]]]**.
  * **tiro**  - Esto es, " ordenar a sus guardias poner. " tenga en cuenta "tirar" como fjue traducido en el cuadro [29-06]
