===== The People Were Hungry [30-04]/ Las personas tenían hambre [30-04] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-04.jpg?nolink&}}

**Late in the day**, the **disciples** told **Jesus**, “It is late and there are no towns nearby. **Send the people away** so they can go get something to eat.”

Al fin del día, los discípulos le dijeron a Jesús: “Ya es tarde y no hay pueblos cerca. Despide a la gente para que puedan ir a buscar algo de comer.”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Late in the day**  - This could also be translated as, "When it was almost evening."
  * **Tarde en el dia **- Esto puede ser traducido como, "cuando ya era tarde"
  * **Send the people away**  - This could be translated as, "Please tell the people to go to a town" or, "Shouldn't you tell the people to go somewhere?" or, "Let the people go to a town." Make sure it sounds like a polite request, not an order.
  * **los mando que se fueran **- Esto puede ser traducido como, " Favor de irse a el pueblo" o, no deberian decirle a la gente que se vayan a algun lugar?" o, " Dejen que la gente se vaya a el pueblo, " Asegurese que suene como una peticion amable, o, como una orden.
