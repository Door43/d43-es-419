===== Assyria Destroyed Israel [20-02]/Asiria Destruyó a Israel [20-02] F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-02.jpg?nolink&}}

So **God punished both kingdoms** by allowing their enemies to destroy them. The **Assyrian Empire**, a powerful, cruel nation, destroyed the **kingdom of Israel**. The Assyrians killed many people in the kingdom of Israel, **took away** everything of value, and burned much of the country.

Así que** Dios castigó a ambos reinos** al permitir que sus enemigos los destruyeran a ellos. El **Imperio Asirio**, una poderosa, cruel nación, destruyó el **reino de Israel**. Los Asírios mataron a mucha gente en el reino de Israel, se llevaron todas las cosas de valor, y quemaron gran parte del país.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **both kingdoms**  - This refers to both the kingdoms of Israel and Judah.
  * **ambos reinos - **  Esto se refiere a ambos reinos de Israel y Judá.
  * **Empire**  - A country was called an "empire" when it was powerful enough to extend its authority over many other countries.
  * **Imperio - **  Un país era llamado un "imperio" cuando era lo suficientemente poderoso para extender su autoridad sobre muchos países.
  * **took away**  - That is, "stole." They stole these valuable things and carried them back to Assyria.
  * **se llevaron**  - Eso es, "robado"**.**  Ellos robaron cosas con valor y se las llevaron para Asiria.
