===== Joseph Was Sold as a Slave [08-02] José fue vendido como un Esclavo [08-02]=====

   

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-02.jpg?nolink&}}

**Joseph’s** brothers hated him because their father loved him most and because Joseph had **dreamed** that he would be their ruler. When Joseph **came to his brothers**, they **kidnapped** him and sold him to some **slave traders**.

Los hermanos de **José** le odiaban porque su padre le amaba a **José** más y porque **José** había **soñado** que él iba a ser su soberano. Cuando **José** llegó a sus hermanos, ellos le secuestraron y le vendieron a algunos traficantes de **esclavos**.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **came to his brothers**  – Another way this could be translated is, "reached the place where his brothers were."
  * **vino a sus hermanos**- Otra manera que esto puede ser traducido es, "llego al lugar donde estaban sus hermanos."
  * **kidnapped**  - They took him against his will. It was not right for them to do this.
  * **secuestrado-**  Ellos se lo llevaron contra su voluntad. No era correcto que ellos hicieran esto.
  * **slave traders**  - These were people who made a business of buying people from one owner and selling them as slaves to another owner.
  * **comerciante de esclavos - **  Estos eran personas que hacían un negocio comprando personas de un dueño y los vendían como esclavos a otro dueño
