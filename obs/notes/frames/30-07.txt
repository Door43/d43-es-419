===== Jesus Blessed the Food [30-07]  Jesús Bendijo los Alimentos [30-07] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **Jesus** took the five loaves of bread and the two fish, looked up to **heaven**, and thanked **God** for the food.

Entonces, Jesús tomó los cinco panes y los dos peces, alzó la mirada al cielo y dio gracias a Dios por la comida.
