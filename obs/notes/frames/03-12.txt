===== Dry Land [03-12] **Tierra Seca**  =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-12.jpg?nolink&}}

**Noah** **waited another week** and sent out the dove a third time. This time, it found a place to rest and did not come back. The water was drying up!

**Noe espero otra semana y mando una paloma afuera por la tercera vez. Esta ves encontro un sitio para descansar y no regreso para atras.**
===== Important Terms: =====

**Terminos Importantes:**

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:noah|Noah]]**
  * **Noe**
===== Translation Note: =====

**Notas Traducidas:**
  * **waited another week**  - You could say, "waited seven more days." The word "waited" shows that Noah was allowing time for the floodwaters to go down before sending out the dove again.
  * **Espero una semana mas - Usted puede decir, "espero siete dias mas." El palabra"espero" ensena que Noe estaba permitiendo mas tiempo para que las aguas del diluvio bajaran antes de mandar la paloma de nuevo.**
