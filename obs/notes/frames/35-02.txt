===== The Religious Leaders Grumbled [35-02] / Los Lideres Religiosos se Quejaron =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-02.jpg?nolink&}}

Some **religious leaders** who were also there saw **Jesus** treating these **sinners** as friends, and they began to criticize him to each other. So Jesus told them this **story**.

Algunos líderes religiosos que estuvieron allí también, vieron a Jesús tratando a los pecadores como amigos, y ellos empezaron a criticarle uno al otro. Así, Jesús les contó esta historia.
