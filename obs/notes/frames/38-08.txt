===== You Will Be Scattered [38-08] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-08.jpg?nolink&}}

After the meal, **Jesus** and his **disciples** walked to the **Mount of Olives**. Jesus said, “You will all **abandon me** tonight. **It is written**, ‘**I will strike the shepherd and all the sheep will be scattered**.’”

Después de la cena, Jesús y sus discípulos caminaron al Monte de los Olivos. Jesús dijo, “Todos Uds. me abandonarán esta noche. Está escrito, “Mataré al pastor y las ovejas serán esparcidas.”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Mount of Olives**  - This is the name of a hill covered with olive trees, just outside the walls of the city of Jerusalem. It can also be translated as, "Olive Tree Hill."
  * **Monte de Olivos - Este es el nombre de la colina cubierta con arboles de olivo, en las afueras de las paredes de la ciudad de Jerusalen.**
  * **abandon me**  - That is, "desert me" or, "leave me."
  * **abandonarme - Esto es, "me abondonas" o, "me dejas."**
  * **It is written**  - That is, "It is written in God's word" or, "It is written in the scriptures" or, "One of God's prophets wrote." It is also possible to say, "What is written will happen" or, "It will be like what is written." This prophecy refers to Jesus' death and his followers running away
  *  \\ **"Esta escrito, Esto es, "Esta escrito en la palabra de Dios" o, "Esta escrito en las escrituras, o, "Uno de los profetas de Dios escribio. "Es posible decir tambien, "Estaba escrito que iba a pasar" o, "Va a hacer como se escribio." La profecia se refiere a la muete de Jesus y sus seguidores huyendo."**
  * **I will strike**  - That is, "I will kill.
  *
  *
  * **Yo herire - Esto es, "Yo matare.**
  * **the shepherd and all the sheep**  - Do not use Jesus' name in this quote because the prophet who first wrote it would not have known the shepherd's name. Also, do not refer to the sheep being the disciples. It is best to use words that mean "shepherd" and "sheep" in your translation.
  * **el pastor y todas sus ovejas - No uses el nombre de Jesus es esta cita porque el profeta que lo escribio primero no iba a saber el nombre del pastor. Tambien, no refieras a los discipulos como ovejas, Es mejor usar la palabra que significa "pastor" y "oveja" en tu traduccion.**
  * **will be scattered**  - That is, "will go away in different directions."
  * van a hacer dispersados - Esto es, se iran en diferentes direcciones."
