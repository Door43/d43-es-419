===== Worship Only God [25-07]  Adorarás solo a Dios =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-07.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** replied, “**Get away from me**, **Satan**! **In God’s word he commands his people**, ‘**Worship only the Lord your God and only serve him**.’”

Entonces, Satanás le mostró a Jesús todos los reinos del mundo y toda su gloria y le dijo: “Te daré todo esto si te postras y me adoras.”

===== Translation Notes =====

  * **In God's word he commands his people, "Worship only the Lord your God and only serve him"**  - This could be written as an indirect quote, "In God's word, he commands us that we must worship and serve only the Lord God, who rules over us."
  * **En palabras de Dios el le ordena su gente, "Alaba unicamente a el Senor tu Dios y siervele a el unicamente" **-Esto puede ser escrito como una orden directa, "En palabra de Dios, el nos ordena que debemos alabar y servir a el Senor Dios, quien reina sobre nosotros."
  * **Lord your God**  - Translate this phrase the same way as you did in **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:25-05|[25-05]]]**.
  * **Senor tu Dios **- Traduce esta frase de la misma manera que usted hizo en **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:25-05|[25-05]]]**.
  * **and only serve him**  - Another way to say this would be, "He is the only one you should serve."
  * **y solamente adora a el **- Otra forma de decir esto es, "El es el unico que tu debes servir"
