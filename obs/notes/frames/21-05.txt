===== The New Covenant [21-05]/El Pacto Nuevo [21-05] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Through the **prophet Jeremiah**, **God promised** that he would make a **New Covenant**, **but not like** the **covenant** God made with **Israel** at **Sinai**. In the New Covenant, God would **write his law on the people’s hearts**, the people would **know God** personally, they would **be his people**, and God would **forgive** their **sins**. The **Messiah** would **start the New Covenant**.

A través del profeta Jeremías, Dios prometió que iba a hacer un pacto nuevo, pero no como el pacto que Dios hizo con Israel en el Sinaí. En el Nuevo Pacto, Dios escribiría su ley en el corazón del pueblo, el pueblo que conocería a Dios personalmente, ellos serían su pueblo, y Dios les perdonaría sus pecados. El Mesías iba a comenzar el Nuevo Pacto.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **Through the prophet Jeremiah God promised**  – This could be translated as, "Through the messages that God gave to Jeremiah, God promised" or, "The prophet Jeremiah told the people God's promise."
  * **A traves del profeta Jeremias Dios prometio** - Esto se puede traducir como, "A traves de los mensajes que Dios le dio a Jeremias, Dios prometio" o, "El profeta Jeremias le dijo a la gente la promesa de Dios."
  * **but not like**  - The New Covenant would be truly effective. People really would know God, they would truly live as his people, and He would forgive their sins completely, based on the Messiah's sacrifice of himself one time for all who believe in him
  * **pero no igual** - El Nuevo Pacto en verdad era efectivo. La gente en realidad iban a conocer a Dios, ellos en verdad vivirian como su gente, y El le perdonaria sus pecados completamente, basado en el sacrificio del mismo Mesias una ves por todas todos los que creian en el.
  * **write his law on the people's hearts**  - This is a metaphor meaning, "help his people to understand his laws and to earnestly desire to obey them." If possible, keep the image of writing on their hearts, since this is in contrast with the way God had written his law on stone tablets for the Israelites. If that is not possible, you could just translate the meaning.
  * **escribe la ley en los corazones de la gente** - Esta metafora significa, "ayudar su gente para entender su leyes y para seriamente desear de obedecerlas." Si es posible , guarda la imagen de escribir en sus corazones, ya que esto es contraste con la forma Dios ha escrito su ley en tabletas de piedra para los Israelitas. Si esto no es posible, tu puedes solamemente traducir el significado.
  * **be his people**  - This could be translated as, "be his special people" or, "be his favored people."
  * **se su gente** - Esto se puede traducir como, "se su gente especial" o, "se su gente favoresida."
  * **start the New Covenant**  - That is, "be the one to cause the New Covenant to take effect" or, "bring the New Covenant to his people."
  * **empieza el Nuevo Pacto** - Esto es, "se uno de los que causa el Nuevo Pacto coja efecto" o, "trae el Nuevo Pacto a su gente."
