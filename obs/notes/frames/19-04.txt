===== God Provided for the Widow [19-04] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-04.jpg?nolink&}}

So **Elijah** went to a **neighboring country**. A widow and her son in that country had almost run out of food because of **the famine**. But they **took care of** Elijah, and **God provided for them so that their flour jar and their bottle of oil never became empty**. They had food through the whole famine. Elijah stayed there for several years.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **neighboring country**  - This refers to a country that was beside, or shared a border with Israel.
  * **pais vecino - Esto se refiere a un pais que esta al lado, o comparte el borde con Israel.**
  * **the famine**  - If needed, this could be translated as, "the famine caused by the drought."
  * **la hambruna - Si es necesario, esto se puede traducir como, "el hambre causado por la sequia."**
  * **took care of**  – This means they gave him a place to stay in their house and provided food for him. It does not mean he was sick.
  *
  * **cuido**** de - esto significa le dieron un sitio para quedarse en su casa y dieron comida. Esto no significa que el estaba enfermo **.
  * **God provided for them…never became empty**  - This could be translated as, "God prevented their flour jar and their bottle of oil from becoming empty" or, "God caused…to never become empty."
  * **Dios les dio a ellos... - nunca se vacio - Esto se puede traducir como, "Dios impedio que el jarra de harina y la botella de aceite no se acabara o, "Dios causo...que nunca estuviera vacia."**
  * **flour jar**  - This refers to a clay jar in which the widow kept her supply of flour
  * .**jarra de harina - Esto se refiere al jarro de harina en que la viuda mantuvo su provision de harina**
  *
  *
  * In Israel, olive oil is used for cooking. This could be translated as, "bottle of cooking oil." The widow used the flour and the oil for making bread.
  * **En Israel, aceite de oliva era usado para cocinar. esto se puede traducir como, "como la botellla de aceite para cocinar" La viuda uso la harina y el aceite para hacer pan.**
