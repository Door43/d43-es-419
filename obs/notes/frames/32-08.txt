===== The Pigs Drowned [32-08] =====

<font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** Los Cerdos Ahogados [32-08]  **</font>

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-08.jpg?nolink&}}

The **demons** came out of the man and entered the pigs. The pigs ran down a steep bank into the lake and drowned. There were about 2,000 pigs in the **herd**.

Los **demonios ** salieron del hombre y entraron el los cerdos. Los cerdos corrieron por un despeñadero al lago y se ahogaron. Había como 2.000 cerdos en la manada.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **herd**  - That is, "herd of pigs" or, "group of pigs." Many languages have specific names for groups of animals, such as "flock of sheep," "herd of cattle," "pack of dogs," and "school of fish." Use a word that can appropriately be used for a large group of pigs.
  * **manada **- Esto es, "manada de cerdos" o, "grupo de cerdos" Algunos idiomas tienen un nombre especifico para grupos de animales, tales como "rebaño de obejas"  "manada o ganado," "paquete de perros," y "escuela de pescados."Use una palabra que pueda ser apropiadamente usada para un largo grupo de cerdos.
