===== Jesus Left [32-10] =====

<font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** Jesús se marchó [32-10]  **</font>

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-10.jpg?nolink&}}

The **people** were very **afraid** and asked **Jesus** to leave. So Jesus got into the boat and **prepared** to leave. The man who used to have the demons **begged to go along with Jesus**.

La gente tenía miedo y **pidió**a **Jesús ** que se fuera. Así, Jesús subió al bote y se preparó para irse. El hombre que antes tenía los demonios rogaba a Jesús permiso para ir con él.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **people**  - Some languages may prefer to say, "people from that region" or, "the Gerasene people."
  * **gente **- Algunas lenguas preferirian decir, "genten de otra region" o, " la gente Gerasene."
  * **afraid**  - It may be better to say, "afraid because of what Jesus had done."
  * **miedo **- Podria ser mejor decir, "con miedo debido a lo que Jesús ha hecho."
  * **prepared**  - That is, "was getting ready."
  * preparado -Esto es, "se preparaba."
  * **begged to go along with Jesus**  - That is, "begged Jesus to let him go with him" or, "earnestly asked Jesus if he could go with him."
  * **pedia ir solo con Jesús **- Esto es,"suplicaba a Jesús le dejara ir con el" o, "seriamente úpidio a Jesus si elm podia ir con el,"
