===== Help for the Victim [27-09] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-09.jpg?nolink&}}

“The **Samaritan** then lifted the man onto **his own donkey** and took him to a **roadside inn where he took care of him**.”

“El Samaritano luego levantó al hombre en su propio burro y lo llevó a un mesón y cuidó de él.”

===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:samaria|Samaritan]]**
===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues to tell the story.)
  * **(Jesus continua para decir la historia.)**
  * **his own donkey**  - Make sure it is clear that "his" refers to the Samaritan.
  * **su propio burro - Asegurate que este claro que "el" se refiere al Samaritano.**
  * **roadside inn**  - That is, "a lodging place." This is a place where travelers could get food and stay overnight
  * **posada en la carretera - Esto es "un sitio de alojamiento**. **"Este es un sitio que los viajeros podian conseguir comida y quedarse la noche**
  * **where he took care of him**  - This could also be translated as, "where he continued to take care of him."
  * **sitio donde cuido de el - Esto se puede traducir tambien como, "donde el continuo cuidandolo a el"**
