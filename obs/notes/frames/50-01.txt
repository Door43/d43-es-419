===== The Church Is Growing [50-01]La Iglesia esta Creciendo [50-01] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-01.jpg?nolink&}}

For almost 2,000 years, more and more people around the world have been hearing the **good news** about **Jesus** the **Messiah**. **The Church has been growing**. Jesus **promised** he would return **at the end of the world**. Though he has not yet come back, he will keep his promise.

Por casi 2,000 años, más y más personas alrededor del mundo han escuchado las buenas nuevas acerca de Jesús el Mesías. La Iglesia ha estado creciendo. Jesús prometió que él volvería al fin del mundo. Aunque no ha vuleto, él cumpirá con su promesa.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Church has been growing**  - This could also be translated as, "The number of people in the Church around the world has been increasing" or, "The number of believers in Jesus has been increasing."
  * **La iglesia a estado creciendo - ** Esto tambien puede ser traducido como, "El numero de gente en la iglesia alrededor del mundo a estado creciendo" o, "El numero de creyientes en Jesus a estado creciendo."
  * **at the end of the world**  - This phrase means, "right before the present world comes to an end" or, "in the final days of this world."
  * **al fin del mundo - ** Esta frase significa, "justo antes que el mundo presente viene a su fin" o, "en los dias finales de este mundo."
