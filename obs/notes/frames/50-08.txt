===== The Field Is the World [50-08]El Campo representa el Mundo [50-08] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-08.jpg?nolink&}}

The **disciples** did not understand the meaning of the story, so they asked **Jesus** to explain it to them. Jesus said, “The man who planted the **good** seed represents the **Messiah**. The field represents the world. The good seed represents **the people of God’s kingdom**.”

Los discípulos no entendieron el sentir de la historia, así, ellos preguntaron a Jesús para que les explcara. Jesús dijo, “El hombre que sembró la semilla buena representa al Mesías. El campo representa el mundo. La semilla buena representa la gente del reino de Dios.”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the people of God's kingdom**  - This can also be translated as, "the people who live under God's authority" or, "the people who have submitted to God's rule" or, "the people who will live with God in his Kingdom."
  * **la gente de el reino de Dios -**  Esto tambien puede ser traducido como, "la gente que vive bajo la autoridad de Dios" o, "la gente que se han sometido al governado de Dios"
