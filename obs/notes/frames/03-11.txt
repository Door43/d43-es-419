===== The Dove [03-11] La Paloma [03-11] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-11.jpg?nolink&}}

Later, **Noah** sent out a bird called a **dove**. But it also could not find any dry land, so it came back to Noah. A week later he sent the dove out again, and it came back with an **olive branch** in its beak! **The water was going down**, and the plants were growing again!

**Despues, Noe mando afuera a un pajaro llamado paloma. Pero no pudo encontrar ninguna tierra seca,  asi que regreso para atras a Noe. Una semana despues el mando la paloma afuera otra vez, y vino para atras con una rama de oliva en el pico! El agua seguia bajando, y las plantas estaban creciendo otra vez.**

===== Translation Notes: =====

**Notas Traducidas:**
  * **dove**  - A small, white or gray flying bird that eats seeds or fruit.
  * **paloma - Un pajaro pequeno, blanca o gris volando que come semillas y frutas.**
  * **olive branch**  - An olive tree's fruit contains oil which people use for cooking or putting on their skin. If your language does not have a word for "olive branch," you could translate it as, "branch from a tree called 'olive'" or, "branch from an oil-tree."
  * **rama de oliva - Un arbol de oliva que su fruto contiene aceite que la gente usa para concinar o se lo ponen en la piel.****Si en ****tu idioma no existe para la palabra "rama de oliva,"** **tu puedes traducirla como, " rama de un arbol llamado 'olivo'****o "rama del arbol de aceite."**
  * **water was going down**  - It may be more natural in your language to say that, "the water was going away" or, "the level of the water was getting lower."
  * **el agua estaba cayendo - Seria mas natura en tu idioma decir que, el agua se estaba alejando" o "el nivel del agua estaba disminuyendo."**
