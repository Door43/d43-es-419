===== The Brothers' Lie [08-03] =====

<font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** La Mentira de los Hermanos [08-03]  **</font>

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-03.jpg?nolink&}}

Before **Joseph’s** brothers returned home, they tore Joseph’s robe and dipped it in **goat's blood**. Then they showed the robe to their father so he would think that a wild animal had killed Joseph. **Jacob** was very sad.

Antes de que los hermanos de **José** volvieron a casa, ellos rompieron la túnica de **José** y la sumergieron en la sangre de una cabra. Entonces, le mostraron la túnica a su padre para que pensara que un animal silvestre había matado a **José**. **Jacob ** estaba muy triste.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **goat's blood**  - The brothers wanted Jacob to think that the blood on the clothes was Joseph's blood.
  * **sangre de una cabra -**  Los hermanos desearon que Jacob pensara que la sangre en la ropa era la sangre de José.
