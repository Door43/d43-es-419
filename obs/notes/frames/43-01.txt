===== The Disciples in Jerusalem [43-01] / Los Discipulos en Jerusalem =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-01.jpg?nolink&}}

After **Jesus returned to heaven**, the **disciples stayed in Jerusalem** as Jesus had commanded them to do. The **believers** there constantly gathered together to **pray**.

Después de volver al cielo Jesús, los discípulos quedaban en Jesursalen como Jesús les había mandado. Los creyentes estaban constantemente juntos para orar.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **returned to heaven**  - That is, "went back to heaven."
  * **volver al cielo**  - Esto es, " ir de nuevo al cielo."
  * **stayed in Jerusalem**  - That is, "stayed in Jerusalem for awhile." They didn't stay there permanently.
  * en Jerusalem - Esto es, "quedarse en Jerusalem por un tiempo." Esllos no se quedaron alli para siempre.
