===== I Am the Son of God [39-04] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-04.jpg?nolink&}}

Jesus said, “**I am**, and you will see me **seated with God and coming from heaven**.” The **high priest tore his clothes in anger** and shouted to the other **religious leaders**, “We do not need any more **witnesses**! You have heard him say that he is the **Son of God**. **What is your judgment**?”

Jesús dijo, “Yo soy, y Ud. me verá sentado con Dios y viniendo del cielo.” El sumo sacerdote partió su ropa de enojo y gritó a los otros líderes religiosos, “¡No necesitamos ningún otro testigo!”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I am**  - That is, "I am as you said" or, "I am the Messiah and the Son of God." "I am" is also the name of God (See **[[:es-419:obs:notes:frames:09-14|[09-14]]]**). By saying simply, "I am" Jesus was also saying that he is God. If possible, translate this so that people will see a similarity between Jesus' answer and the name of God.
  * **Yo soy - Esto es, "Yo soy lo que dijistes" o, "Yo soy el Mesias y el Hijo de Dios," Yo soy" es tambien el nombre de Dios (Vea [09-14]**. **Diciendo simplemente, "Yo soy" Jesus tambien estaba diciendo que el era Dios. Si es posible, ** traduce esto** asi otra gente ve la similaridad entre la contesta de Jesus y el nombre de Dios.**
  * **seated with God**  - This could be translated as, "reigning with God." Because God is the ruler over all, people talk about him as sitting on a throne in heaven. By saying that he would be seated with God, Jesus claimed that he had authority to rule with the Father.
  * **sentado con Dios - Esto puede ser traducido como, "reinando con Dios." Porque Dios es el soberano sobre todo, la gente habla sobre el estar sentado en un trono en el cielo. Diciendo ** que el va **a estar sentado con Dios, Jesus reclama que el tiene autoridad ****para gobernar con**  su Padre.
  * **seated with God and coming from heaven**  - This could be translated as, "sitting beside God and then coming from heaven."
  * **tore his clothes in anger**  - The Jews would tear their clothes to show grief or anger. If tearing the clothes means something else in your language, you may want to substitute a phrase such as, "he was extremely angry.
  * **rompio su ropa en ira - Los Judios desgarraban su ropa  para mostrar dolor o ira. Si desgarrando la ropa significa algo diferente en tu idioma, es posible que desee substituir la frase tal como, "el estaba extremademente enojado.**
  * **What is your judgment?**  - That is, "What is your decision?" or, "Tell us what have you decided: Is he innocent or guilty?" The chief priest wanted the religious leaders to condemn Jesus for claiming to be equal with God.
  * **Cual es tu juicio?  - Esto es, "Cual es tu decision? o, "Dinos que has decidido: **El es inocente o culpable?" El sacerdote principal queria que los lideres religiosos condenaran a Jesus por reclamar que el es igual a Dios.** **
