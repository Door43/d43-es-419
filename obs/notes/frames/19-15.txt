===== Naaman Was Healed [19-15]/Naaman Fue Sanado [10-15] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-15.jpg?nolink&}}

At first **Naaman** was angry and **would not do it because it seemed foolish**. But later **he changed his mind** and dipped himself seven times in the **Jordan River**. When he came up the last time, his skin was completely **healed**! **God** had healed him.

Al principio Naamán se enojó, y no lo haría porque le parecía una tontería. Pero más tarde cambió de opinión y se sumergió siete veces en el río Jordán. Cuando subió la última vez, su piel estaba completamente curado! Dios lo había sanado.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **would not do it because it seemed foolish**  - Naaman would not do what Elisha said because he knew that washing alone could not heal his disease.
  * **no lo queria hacer porque parecia tonto -**  Naaman no queria hacer lo que Eliseo le dijo porque el sabia que lavarse solamente no podia curar la enfermedad.
  * **he changed his mind**  - That is, "he decided to do what Elisha had told him to do."
  * **el cambio su mente - ** Esto es, "el decidio hacer lo que Eliseo le dijo que hiciera."
