===== Pharaoh Chased Israel [12-04]Faraon persiguio a Israel [12-04] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-04.jpg?nolink&}}

So **Pharaoh** and his army chased after the **Israelites** to **make them their slaves again**. When the Israelites saw the **Egyptian** army coming, they realized **they were trapped between Pharaoh's army and the Red Sea**. They were very afraid and cried out, “**Why did we leave Egypt**? We are going to die!”

Así, el **Faraón** y su ejercito persiguió a los** Israelitas** para hacerles sus **esclavos** otra vez. Cuando los **Israelitas** vieron al ejercito de Egipto acercándose, se dieron que estaban atrapados entre el ejercito del **Faraón** y el **Mar Rojo**. Ellos tenían miedo y clamaron, “¿Por qué salimos de** Egipto**? ¡Vamos a morir!”

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **they were trapped between…Red Sea**  - Another way to say this would be, "there was nowhere they could go to escape since the Egyptians were behind them and the Red Sea was in front of them.
  * **estaban atrapados entre…Mar Rojo** - Otra forma de decir esto puede ser, "no habia ninguna parte que ellos podian escaparse debido a que los egipcios estaban detras de ellos y el Mar Rojo estaba al frente de ellos.
  * **Why did we leave Egypt?**  – This means, "We should not have left Egypt!" They were not really asking for reasons. Because they were afraid, in this moment they were wishing they had not left Egypt (even though it had been very difficult for them there).
  * **Porque nosotros salimos de Egipto?** - Esto significa, "Nosotros no debimos salir de Egipto!"Ellos no se estaban preguntando las razones. Porque ellos tenian miedo, en ese momento ellos estaban deseando que nunca salieron de Egipto (a pesar que habia sido bien dificil para ellos ahi)
