===== What Is Our Reward? [28-09] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-09.jpg?nolink&}}

**Peter** said to **Jesus**, “We have **left everything** and followed you. **What will be our reward**?”

Pedro dijo a Jesús: “Nosotros hemos dejado todo y hemos seguido a usted. ¿Cuál será nuestra recompensa?”

===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:peter|Peter]]**
  * **[[:es-419:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**
===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **left everything**  - That is, "left everything behind" or, "given up everything we owned."
  * **dejamos todo - Esto es, "dejamos todo atras" o, "renunciamos a todo lo que teniamos."**
  * **What will be our reward?**  - Another way to say this is, "What reward will we receive" or, "How will we be rewarded?" or, "What will God give us as a reward?" It may also be necessary to add, "because we have done this?"
  * **Cual sera nuestra recompensa? - Otra forma de decir esto es, "Que recompesa vamos a recibir" o, "Como vamos a hacer recompensados? " o, "Que Dios nos dara a nosotros como recompensa?"Puede ser necesario anadir,"Porque nosotros hicimos esto?"**
