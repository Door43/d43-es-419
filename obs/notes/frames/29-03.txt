===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * **pay the debt**  - That is, "pay back the money he owed to the king."
  * **pagar la deuda**
 -Esto es, "pagar el dinero nque el le debe al rey."
  * **to make payment on his debt**  - This could be translated as, "and use the money received from selling them to pay part of what he owes me."
  * **hacer pagos a sus deudas **- Esto puede ser traducido como, " y usar parte de el dinero reciviso de su venta para pagarme parte de lo que me deben.
