===== The Earthquake [47-09] =====

Suddenly, there was a violent earthquake! All the prison doors **flew open**, and the chains of all the prisoners **fell off**.

Â¡De repente, hubo un teremoto violento! Toda las puertas de la carcel se abrieron, y las cadenas de los prisioneros se cayeron al suelo.

===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **flew open**  - That is, "became unlocked and suddenly opened wide."
  * **volo abierta - Esto es, "se convirtio en desbloqueado y de repente abierta."**
  * **fell off**  - That is, "suddenly came off" or, "suddenly came apart so that the prisoners were released."
  * **cayo - Esto es, "de repente se desprendio o, "de repente vino aparte asi que los prisioneros fueron liberados."**
