===== God Answered with Fire [19-11]/Dios Respondio con Fuego [19-11] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-11.jpg?nolink&}}

Immediately, fire **fell from the sky** and burned up the meat, the wood, the rocks, the dirt, and even the water that was around the **altar**. When the people saw this, they **fell on the ground** and said, “**Yahweh is God**! Yahweh is God!”

Inmediatamente, el fuego cayó del cielo y quemó la carne, la madera, las rocas, la tierra, e incluso el agua que estaba en torno al altar. Cuando el pueblo vio esto, se postraron en el suelo y dijo: “¡Jehová es Dios! ¡Jehová es Dios!”

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **fell from the sky**  - This may be translated as, "suddenly came down from the sky."
  * **cayo del cielo - ** Esto puede ser traducido como, "de repente descendio del cielo."
  * **fell on the ground**  - They quickly lay down or knelt down on the ground. They were afraid of Yahweh because they had seen his power. They knew that only the true God could do that and bowed down to honor and worship him.
  * **cayo al suelo -**  Ellos rapidamente se inclinaron en el suelo. Ellos tenian miedo de Jesus porque ellos habian visto su poder. Ellos sabian que el era el unico verdadero Dios que podia hacer eso y hicieron reverencia para honrarlo y alabarlo.
  * **Yahweh is God**  - This expression means that they understood that Yahweh is the only God, not just one god among others.
  * **Jesus is Dios - ** Esta expresion significa que ellos entendieron que Jesus es el unico Dios, no uno entre los otros.
